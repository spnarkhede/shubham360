## ğŸ§  Theory & Grammar

### 1. Prepositions with Accusative/Dative
Some prepositions always take a specific case:

- **Accusative only:** um, bis
- **Dative only:** ab, seit, nach, vor, zwischen (if static), in, an (when referring to time)
