# Cheap Airlines Prompts

A collection of AI prompts to help find the best flight deals, optimize routes, and reduce travel costs.

---

### 1. Deal and Discount Checker

**Use Case:** Quickly surface valid promo codes and flash offers before booking a flight, avoiding expired or fake deals.

**Target Personas:** Budget Traveller, Frequent Traveller, Deal Hunter

**Tags:** `Budget Traveller`, `Travel`, `Research`

**Prompt:**

```
Search for active promo codes, flash offers, and airline discounts for `[airline names]`. Verify expiration dates and legitimacy, and remove any offers that are outdated or unconfirmed.
```

---

### 2. Hidden Flight Finder

**Use Case:** Discovering cheaper flights that don't appear in standard search results, including regional carriers and unconventional routes.

**Target Personas:** Budget Traveller, Frequent Traveller, Independent Traveller

**Tags:** `Budget Traveller`, `Travel`, `Research`

**Prompt:**

```
Find all available flights from `[origin]` to `[destination]` over the next `[X weeks]`, including budget airlines, regional carriers, and less obvious connecting routes. Rank them by total final cost, not just the starting base fare.
```

---

### 3. Ideal Date Scanner

**Use Case:** Identifying the cheapest travel date combinations within a flexible window around a target trip date.

**Target Personas:** Budget Traveller, Flexible Traveller, Frequent Traveller

**Tags:** `Budget Traveller`, `Travel`, `Planning`

**Prompt:**

```
Analyze the most economical departure and return dates within a range of `[X days]` around the `[target dates]`. Compare combinations and justify which are the 3 best options with detailed reasoning.
```

---

### 4. Route Optimizer with Smart Connections

**Use Case:** Finding cost-effective multi-stop routes when direct flights exceed the budget, while minimising layover time and transit fees.

**Target Personas:** Budget Traveller, Business Traveller, Frequent Traveller

**Tags:** `Budget Traveller`, `Travel`, `Planning`

**Prompt:**

```
Design alternative routes with 1 or 3 connections from `[Origin]` to `[Destination]` for less than `$[X]`. Prioritize connections with less than `[X hours]` layover and airports without high transit fees.
```

---

### 5. Verified Deals and Promotions Detector

**Use Case:** Validating current airline promotions before booking to ensure codes are active and sourced from legitimate channels.

**Target Personas:** Budget Traveller, Deal Hunter, Frequent Traveller

**Tags:** `Budget Traveller`, `Travel`, `Research`

**Prompt:**

```
Find active promotional codes, flash sales, and discounts for flights with `[airlines]`. Check the expiration date and source. Discard those that are expired or cannot be verified.
```

---

### 6. Breakdown and Elimination of Extra Fees

**Use Case:** Understanding and legally minimising ancillary charges (baggage, seat selection, priority boarding) before or after booking.

**Target Personas:** Budget Traveller, Frequent Traveller, Business Traveller

**Tags:** `Budget Traveller`, `Travel`, `Cost Optimisation`

**Prompt:**

```
Detail all additional fees for this flight — baggage, seats, priority boarding — and suggest legal strategies to avoid or minimize each of them, based on current fare rules.
```

---

### 7. Price Negotiation Email

**Use Case:** Drafting a compelling email to an airline requesting a price match or discount application, backed by policy and loyalty data.

**Target Personas:** Frequent Traveller, Business Traveller, Corporate Travel Manager

**Tags:** `Business Traveller`, `Travel`, `Communication`

**Prompt:**

```
Draft a professional and persuasive email requesting a price match with a competitor or the application of a discount. Mention loyalty programs, current pricing policies, and alternative pricing options.
```

---

### 8. Flexibility and Risk Analysis

**Use Case:** Comparing cancellation and change policies across flight options to choose the lowest-risk booking before committing.

**Target Personas:** Business Traveller, Frequent Traveller, Cautious Traveller

**Tags:** `Business Traveller`, `Travel`, `Risk Analysis`

**Prompt:**

```
Analyze the change, cancellation, and refund policies for these flights. Identify which one offers the lowest financial risk should plans change. Highlight any relevant hidden clauses.
```

---

### 9. Strategy for Hidden Destination Tickets

**Use Case:** Evaluating whether a hidden-city ticketing strategy is viable for a specific route, including an honest assessment of the risks involved.

**Target Personas:** Budget Traveller, Frequent Traveller, Independent Traveller

**Tags:** `Budget Traveller`, `Travel`, `Cost Optimisation`

**Prompt:**

```
Evaluate whether hidden destination tickets can reduce the cost from `[origin]` to `[destination]`. Explain the real risks, airline policies, and in which specific cases this approach is worthwhile.
```
