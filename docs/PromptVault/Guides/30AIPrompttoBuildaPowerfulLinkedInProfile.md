# 30 AI Prompts to Build a Powerful LinkedIn Profile 


Your LinkedIn profile is the foundation of your digital identity - a strategic showcase
of your story, visual brand, and professional voice. This document contains tailored
AI prompts to help you define, design, and amplify your presence online.
Whenever you see placeholders like [Your Industry], [Your Job Title], [Your Key
Skills], or [Your Target Audience/Clients], replace them with your own details - to
make every prompt uniquely yours and perfectly aligned with your goals!
To get the most relevant and powerful results, enrich your prompts with context
drawn from your own career journey and achievements - don’t limit yourself to just
the prompts provided. And remember: always review and revise the AI’s output to
ensure it truly reflects you!
1. Define Your Story
This section focuses on refining your LinkedIn headline, “About” section, and
“Experience” details to express clarity, credibility, and personal authenticity.
AI Tools To Use
● ChatGPT: Crafts strong headlines and summaries.
● Claude: Refines tone and storytelling style.
● Gemini: Suggests clear, industry-aligned phrasing.
● Teal: Aligns your profile with career goals.
● Careerflow.ai: Optimises profiles for recruiter visibility.
● Jobscan: Matches your profile to job keywords.
Prompts
Positioning & Introduction
Recommended tools: ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude
1. Headline Optimisation Prompt:
“Write a LinkedIn headline that highlights my role as a [Your Job Title] in [Your
Industry], emphasising [Your Key Expertise] and how I help [Your Target
1
Audience] achieve [Specific Result].”
2. About Section Rewrite Prompt:
“Rewrite my ‘About’ section to share my story as a [Your Job Title] with
[Number of Years] years of experience in [Your Industry]. Focus on my values,
achievements, and mission to help [Your Audience/Clients] succeed.”
3. Professional Summary Prompt:
“Draft a 100-word introduction I can use across my LinkedIn sections that
summarises my expertise in [Your Field] and my goal to contribute to [Target
Outcome or Market].”
4. Value Proposition Prompt:
“Write three short value statements that summarise how I make an impact in
[Your Industry], focusing on [Your Distinct Strengths or Achievements].”
Credentials & Experience
Recommended tools: ChatGPT, Teal, Claude, Grammarly
5. Experience Impact Prompt:
“Transform my work description at [Your Company Name] into dynamic bullet
points that emphasise measurable success and leadership in [Your Area of
Expertise].”
6. Achievement Highlight Prompt:
“Identify five quantifiable results I’ve achieved in [Your Previous Role] and
rewrite them to emphasise performance, efficiency, and leadership impact.”
7. Tone Alignment Prompt:
“Adjust this description to match a tone that is confident yet personable,
appealing to both peers and recruiters in [Your Industry].”
8. Career Story Prompt:
“Align all my experience summaries to tell a cohesive story showing my
professional evolution toward [Your Current Specialisation or Goal].”
Optimisation & Visibility
Recommended tools: Jobscan, Careerflow.ai
9. Profile Audit Checklist Prompt:
“Generate a detailed checklist for reviewing my headline, summary, and
experience for readability, data-backed impact, and narrative consistency.”

10.Keyword Strategy Prompt:

2. Design Your Look
This section develops your visual identity - including profile image, banner, and
media content - to strengthen your credibility and recognition.
AI Tools To Use
● Canva: Designs banners and visual assets.
● DALL‑E: Creates custom AI-generated visuals.
● Midjourney: Produces artistic brand imagery.
● HeadshotPro: Generates professional headshots.
● Looka: Builds cohesive visual branding.
● Remini: Enhances and sharpens photos.
Prompts
Personal Visual Identity
Recommended tools: HeadshotPro, Remini, Canva, Looka
1. Headshot Planning Prompt:
“Describe how I should pose, dress, and light my LinkedIn headshot to project
authority and authenticity as a [Your Job Title] in [Your Industry].”
2. AI Headshot Generation Prompt:
“Create a prompt for [Chosen AI Tool] to generate a professional headshot
background suitable for a [Your Role Type: e.g. Executive/Creative/Tech
Professional].”
3. Banner Design Prompt:
“Design a LinkedIn banner that visually communicates my mission to [Your
Core Career Purpose] using imagery related to [Your Industry Themes].”
4. Personal Branding Palette Prompt:
“Propose a colour scheme and font pairing that reinforce professionalism and
approachability for a [Your Industry] specialist.”

Branded Content and Creative Assets
Recommended tools: Canva, DALL‑E, Midjourney
5. Visual Symbolism Prompt:
“Generate icons or visual symbols that represent my top skills in [Skill 1], [Skill
2], and [Skill 3] for portfolio and featured visuals.”
6. Featured Section Prompt:
“Recommend three types of visual content (certificates, project screenshots,
or infographics) showcasing achievements in [Your Field].”
7. Post Thumbnail Prompt:
“Design a thumbnail mockup style that maintains brand consistency and
draws attention to thought leadership posts in [Your Industry].”
8. Introductory Video Script Prompt:
“Write a 60-second script introducing who I am, my journey in [Your Industry],
and the value I bring as a [Your Role].”
9. Explainer Video Prompt:
“Develop a script or video flow explaining how I help clients achieve [Specific
Result] through [Your Expertise].”
10.Trend Adaptation Prompt:
“List current design trends in [Your Industry] and suggest how I can adapt my
LinkedIn visuals to remain modern and relevant.”
3. Amplify Your Voice
This section builds your content strategy, enabling you to share consistent insights
and strengthen your presence with sustainable visibility.
AI Tools To Use
● ChatGPT: Writes engaging post ideas.
● Jasper: Optimises professional content.
● Copy.ai: Creates short LinkedIn copy.
● Draftly: Plans and schedules posts.
● Taplio: Tracks and boosts engagement.
● Grammarly: Polishes tone and grammar.
● Shield Analytics: Measures LinkedIn performance.

Prompts
Positioning & Introduction

“Build a 4-week content plan for a [Your Role] focusing on educating,

with fellow [Your Industry] professionals.”

8. Engagement Tactics Prompt:
“Suggest ways to boost engagement in [Your Sector] through conversations,
storytelling, videos, collaborations, and community interaction.”

Final Takeaways
A standout LinkedIn presence doesn’t come from a one-time update: it grows
through ongoing clarity, creativity, and consistency.
Use these prompts to refine your story, sharpen your look, and sustain your voice.
Whether you’re starting out or scaling influence, keep customising your content and
visuals as your career evolves.
