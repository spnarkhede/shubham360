To guide our discussion,** consider the following example:** In the city of Portland, Oregon, community engagement led to the development of a light rail system that significantly improved connectivity and reduced traffic congestion. How can we apply similar principles to our local context? To ensure the plan is effective, what are the key steps we should follow, and how can we involve diverse community voices in the decision-making process?
