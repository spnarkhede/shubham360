### What it does

- Generates a clean axonometric AI image of a circular interior space with a central spiral staircase core.

### Tips

- Define the radial zone program by choosing functional spaces that work well in circular layouts, such as library reading nooks, gallery display areas, or collaborative workspaces arranged around the spiral core.
- Select an accent color that reinforces the architectural concept, like deep blue for calm study spaces, warm terracotta for creative studios, or forest green for wellness centers.
- Choose an aspect ratio that showcases the circular geometry clearly, using square format for balanced composition or wide format to emphasize the radial spatial organization.
