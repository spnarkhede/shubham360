> Extract repeatable success principles with this AI prompt, using recursive analysis to identify transferable strategies, key decisions, and actionable protocols.

### Prompt

```
Decompose a business success into phases, decisions, and causal conditions, then rebuild into transferable principles and a repeatable protocol.

# INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My success description: [DESCRIBE]
- My timeline: [KEY DATES]
- My team and resources: [WHO + WHAT]
- My theory about why it worked: [THEORY]
- My differentiators this time: [WHAT WAS DIFFERENT]

MOST IMPORTANT:
Output sections in order:
1) Phase Decomposition
2) Key Decision Register
3) Transferability Sort
4) Transferable Principles (4-7)
5) Repeatable Success Protocol
6) Near-Miss Lessons
7) Adaptation Notes
```

### What it does

- Separates luck and one-time conditions from transferable strategic mechanics.
- Converts successful decisions into actionable reusable rules.
- Builds a protocol another team can execute with minimal ambiguity.

### Tips

- Include counterfactuals to avoid false causality conclusions.
- Favor fewer stronger principles over long generic lists.
- Test protocol on smaller live projects before broad adoption.

### How to use

1. Provide concrete timeline and decision context, not just outcomes.
2. Run prompt and apply protocol to next initiative cycle.
