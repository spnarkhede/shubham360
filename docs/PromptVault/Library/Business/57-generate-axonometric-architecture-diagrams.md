### What it does

- Generates an exploded axonometric AI image that separates a building into eight distinct layers showing structure, walls, floors, and roof.
- Creates a technical architectural diagram with clean line work that displays how building components assemble vertically along a central axis.
- Produces a publication-ready AI image prompt output using precise CAD-style lines without shadows, textures, or perspective distortion.

### Tips

- Choose a specific building type like residential villa, community library, or art gallery to define the spatial hierarchy and circulation logic for your AI image prompt.
- Define your building scale and primary spaces clearly, such as a compact single-family home versus an expansive multi-unit structure, to guide the layer complexity.
- Select a single ink color like deep blue, charcoal gray, or burnt orange that aligns with your publication style and enhances the technical diagram aesthetic of the AI image.
