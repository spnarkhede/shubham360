### What it does

- Generates a clean 3D axonometric AI image of a contemporary institution interior with precise architectural lines.
- Shows spatial organization including exhibition walls, structural systems, and circulation paths without perspective distortion.
- Creates a technical diagram with monochrome linework and minimal shading for portfolio-ready architectural visualization.

### Tips

- Choose an institution type that matches your project needs, such as museum, gallery, or library, and select a center feature like a sculpture court or reading area to anchor the AI image composition.
- Define display objects that suit your institution type, such as art pieces for galleries or artifacts for museums, and pick a line color like dark gray or navy that complements your brand identity.
- Use the JSON prompt to guide the AI in creating a clean axonometric drawing that shows spatial relationships and structural systems clearly for architectural presentations.
