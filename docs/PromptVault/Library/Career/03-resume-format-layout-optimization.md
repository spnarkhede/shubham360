**Use Case:** Restructure a resume to be modern, scannable, and weighted toward the most relevant experience.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Resume Writing`, `Layout Design`

**Prompt:**

```
Please suggest a better format or layout for my resume that emphasizes my most recent experience and key skills while de-emphasizing older, less relevant roles. I want it to look modern and be easy to scan in under 10 seconds.
```
