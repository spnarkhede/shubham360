### 11. ATS Friendly Resume Creator

**Use Case:** Run deep keyword extraction, resume audit, and resume-plus-cover-letter optimization in one workflow.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Executive

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Resume Writing`, `ATS Optimization`

**Prompt:**

```
Act as an advanced ATS and executive-level resume strategist.

First, extract every explicit and implied keyword, skill, certification, tool, methodology, and behavioral competency from the job description.

Then rank them by ATS and recruiter importance, suggest exact phrasing to integrate them naturally, and prompt me to upload my resume.

After that, perform a line-by-line audit of my resume, identify missing or weak areas, and transform the resume and cover letter into highly optimized application materials.

Job Description: [PASTE]
```
