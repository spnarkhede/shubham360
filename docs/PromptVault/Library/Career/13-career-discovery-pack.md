### 1. Career Discovery Prompt

**Use Case:** Generate a broad list of career ideas based on interests, values, strengths, and transferable skills.

**Target Personas:** Student, Career Explorer, Career Changer

**Tags:** `Career Exploration`, `Research`, `Career Planning`

**Prompt:**

```
Based on my interests in [interests], my majors or academic interests in [majors], my values of [values], my strengths in [strengths], and the transferable skills outlined in my resume [paste], suggest 12 potential career ideas.

Weigh all aspects equally and validate the suggestions against the Occupational Outlook Handbook. For each career idea, provide a brief description, common projects or tasks, typical entry-level job titles, and typical companies or work contexts.
```

### 2. Expand Your Options Prompt

**Use Case:** Find alternate career paths related to a current role by balancing strengths, interests, and academic background.

**Target Personas:** Career Changer, Student, Professional

**Tags:** `Career Exploration`, `Transferable Skills`, `Career Planning`

**Prompt:**

```
Suggest 12 alternate career ideas to the role of [role] in the field of [field] that align with my top strengths [strengths]. Also integrate my interests [interests], majors [majors], values [values], and transferable skills from my resume [paste].

Validate each suggested role against the Occupational Outlook Handbook and, for each one, provide a role description, common tasks, entry-level job titles, and likely industries or organizations.
```

### 3. Role Research Prompt

**Use Case:** Understand the daily reality, compensation, and growth path of a target role.

**Target Personas:** Student, Career Explorer, Job Seeker

**Tags:** `Career Research`, `Role Research`, `Career Planning`

**Prompt:**

```
Tell me about the role of [Role] in the field of [Field]. What are typical tasks, what is a typical day like, what impact would I make, how much money would I make, what is the work-life balance like, and what is the career trajectory including further education needed to advance?
```

### 4. Industry Research Prompt

**Use Case:** Research a target industry with validated trends, growth prospects, and reliable resources.

**Target Personas:** Student, Career Explorer, Professional

**Tags:** `Industry Research`, `Career Research`, `Market Trends`

**Prompt:**

```
Help me identify verifiable current industry trends in [field]. Explain growth prospects, emerging topics, and reliable sources I should use. Validate the research with the Occupational Outlook Handbook and other reputable sources, and provide fully displayed source URLs.
```

---

### 5. Alumni Cold Outreach Prompt

**Use Case:** Draft a concise networking message to alumni for career insight conversations.

**Target Personas:** Student, Career Explorer, Job Seeker

**Tags:** `Networking`, `Cold Outreach`, `Career Growth`

**Prompt:**

```
I am a student seeking to network with alumni for career insights. Help me draft a concise cold networking message for [LinkedIn or Email] to an alum working in [field]. Mention why I want to connect, what stage I am in academically, what area I am exploring, and include a clear call to action. Also provide a strong subject line.
```
