> Create stunning fashion lifestyle collages with this AI prompt, featuring consistent characters in customizable outfits across dynamic urban poses and angles.

### Prompt

```
{
  "scene_description": "A cohesive 4-panel fashion lifestyle collage featuring the same [SUBJECT] in a trendy [STYLE AESTHETIC] outfit, showcasing different dynamic poses and angles.",
  "subject": {
    "type": "[SUBJECT] (Consistent character)",
    "age": "early 20s",
    "features": {
      "hair": "high ponytail or messy bun",
      "makeup": "fresh urban look"
    },

    "accessories": "cross-body bag, sunglasses on head"
  },
  "collage_layout": {
    "structure": "2x2 Grid Layout (4 frames of equal size)",
    "panel_1_top_left": "Front Full Body: Walking confidently towards the camera on a city crosswalk, hair moving in the wind, looking slightly to the side.",
    "panel_2_top_right": "Side Profile Sitting: Sitting on concrete steps with knees pulled up, resting chin on knees, looking peacefully at the street view.",
    "panel_3_bottom_left": "Back View Full Body: Standing and looking away at a city billboard or view, hands in hoodie pockets, highlighting the back graphic of the hoodie.",

  }

```

### What it does

- Generates a 4-panel AI image collage showing the same person in different urban poses and angles.
- Creates street fashion photography featuring customizable outfits and brand products in a city setting.
- Produces high-resolution lifestyle shots with consistent character across all four frames in a 2x2 grid layout.

### Tips

- Choose a style aesthetic that matches your brand identity, such as minimalist for clean fashion brands or grunge for edgy streetwear, and select a subject type like female model or male influencer to represent your target audience.
- Customize the environment variable with specific urban settings that align with your product story, such as downtown graffiti walls for street brands or modern glass buildings for tech-forward fashion lines.
- Replace the default attire with your actual product offerings and insert your brand name on visible clothing areas like the hoodie chest or sneakers to showcase your merchandise naturally across all four panels.
