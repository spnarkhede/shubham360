### What it does

- Generates a premium 2×3 grid AI image showcasing a wireless speaker across six lifestyle panels in portrait format.
- Creates professional product photography combining hero shots, macro details, and interior lifestyle scenes with natural lighting.
- Produces an elegant advertisement AI image that highlights sound quality, craftsmanship, and seamless home integration for marketing use.

### Tips

- Choose a memorable product name that reflects your brand identity, select a cohesive material palette like warm woods and soft neutrals or modern metallics and stone, and define interior worlds that match your target lifestyle such as Scandinavian living room, urban loft kitchen, or cozy bedroom reading nook.
- Use the JSON prompt to guide the AI in creating a premium grid advertisement that showcases your wireless speaker across multiple lifestyle scenarios.
- Consider how your brand personality translates to interior settings by matching color palettes to real home environments, ensuring the speaker design complements rather than dominates each scene for authentic lifestyle integration.
