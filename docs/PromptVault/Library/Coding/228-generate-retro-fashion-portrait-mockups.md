### What it does

- Generates an AI image of a subject in a vintage luxury car wearing an elaborate winter outfit with fur and jeweled accessories.
- Creates a retro editorial fashion photograph with soft film-like qualities and warm interior lighting contrasted against cold exterior views.
- Maintains exact facial features from a reference photo while positioning the subject in a regal seated pose with specific styling details preserved.

### Tips

- Choose a cohesive color theme for your AI image prompt by selecting a rich winter tone like burgundy, emerald, or sapphire to fill all outfit theme variables, ensuring the dress, gloves, hat, car interior, and lighting all match for a unified editorial look.
- Select an accent stone that complements your chosen outfit theme, such as ruby for burgundy, emerald for green, or sapphire for blue tones, to create harmonious jewelry details in your AI image.
- Pick a background setting that enhances the winter fashion mood, such as snowy mountain landscape, frosted city street, or winter forest, visible through the car windows to add atmospheric depth to the composition.
