### 3. Tone-Based Cover Letter

**Use Case:** Adapt a cover letter tone to match company culture and hiring context.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Professional, Career Changer

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Writing`, `Cover Letter`

**Prompt:**

```
Write two versions of a cover letter for the same role:
1) Formal corporate tone,
2) Conversational startup tone.

Role: [Job Title]
Company: [Company Name]
Resume: [PASTE]
```
