### 6. Entry-Level and Career Change Cover Letter

**Use Case:** Write a cover letter for a first role or a role change by emphasizing transferable strengths.

**Target Personas:** Recent Graduate, Career Changer, Job Seeker

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Writing`, `Career Change`

**Prompt:**

```
Help me write a cover letter for either:
1) My first job after college, highlighting internships and academic achievements, or
2) A career change into [new field], highlighting transferable skills and motivation.

Resume: [PASTE]
Job description: [PASTE]
```
