### What it does

- Analyzes customer feedback to create internal escalation notes for relevant departments.
- Structures the AI prompt output with subject line, issue summary, customer impact, evidence, support actions, and requested next steps.
- Tailors the escalation message tone and framing to match the receiving team while keeping it under 300 words.

### Tips

- Review past escalation notes that led to quick resolutions to identify which framing approaches and evidence types resonated most with each department, helping you refine your AI prompt inputs over time.
- Create a simple tracking system that logs which escalations generated action within 48 hours versus those that stalled, then analyze patterns to improve how you present customer feedback context in future prompts.
- Schedule a brief follow-up meeting with receiving departments after they act on your escalation to gather their feedback on what information was most useful, which you can then incorporate into your next AI prompt iteration.
