### What it does

- Creates a split-screen luxury product showcase AI image with full product view and detailed close-ups.
- Generates hyper-realistic 8K product photography with customizable material options and premium UI elements.
- Produces an ultra-luxury editorial style AI image perfect for high-end brand presentations and design portfolios.

### Tips

- Choose a luxury product that benefits from material variety, such as watches with leather and metal, pens with wood and gold accents, or tech devices with ceramic and titanium finishes.
- Define your material language by pairing contrasting textures that complement each other, like matte black carbon fiber with polished rose gold trim, or ivory marble with brushed bronze details.
- Create your atmosphere by selecting a cohesive color palette and setting description that matches your brand identity, such as warm amber and cognac tones for a heritage brand or cool slate and platinum for a modern tech aesthetic.
