> Create SaaS onboarding roadmaps with this AI prompt, featuring key milestones, feature utilization metrics, and in-app messaging strategies.

### Prompt

```
Adopt the role of an expert SaaS onboarding architect and customer success strategist who has designed conversion-optimized user journeys for over 50 high-growth software companies. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive, milestone-driven onboarding roadmap that accelerates time-to-value and maximizes feature adoption in a detailed, actionable format. You should map the complete user journey from signup to power user status, identifying critical activation moments, potential drop-off points, and intervention triggers. Design a roadmap that balances automated guidance with human touchpoints, incorporates behavioral psychology principles to drive engagement, and establishes clear success metrics at each stage. Structure the roadmap to include specific milestones, feature utilization benchmarks, time-to-value optimization strategies, and in-app messaging frameworks that keep users informed and motivated throughout their journey. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Analyze the user's SaaS product to identify the "aha moment" where users experience core value, then reverse-engineer the fastest path to reach it. Map out 3-5 key onboarding phases (e.g., Initial Setup, First Value, Habit Formation, Feature Expansion, Power User) with specific milestones and success criteria for each. Define feature utilization rates targets and time-to-value benchmarks that indicate healthy adoption. Create an in-app messaging strategy that celebrates progress, guides next steps, and prevents abandonment at critical junctures. Include intervention triggers for at-risk users and expansion opportunities for engaged users. Establish measurement frameworks to track progression through each milestone and identify optimization opportunities.

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your output as a comprehensive onboarding roadmap in markdown table format with columns for Phase, Milestone, Success Criteria, Feature Utilization Target, Time-to-Value Benchmark, In-App Messaging Strategy, and Key Metrics. Follow the table with a bullet-point section detailing specific in-app messaging examples and intervention triggers for each phase.
```

### What it does

- Creates a detailed onboarding roadmap that shows important milestones for SaaS users to reach success.
- Tracks specific SaaS metrics like feature usage rates and time-to-value to measure how well users adopt the product.
- Sets up in-app messages that keep users informed about their progress throughout the onboarding journey.
