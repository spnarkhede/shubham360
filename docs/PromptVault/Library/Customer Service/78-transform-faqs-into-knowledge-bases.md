> Create structured knowledge base articles with this AI prompt, transforming FAQs into complete guides with context, steps, and support options.

### Prompt

```
Adopt the role of a Customer Self-Service Content Specialist.
Transform short FAQ items into complete, standalone KB articles with structure, context, steps, related links, and contact fallback.

# INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My FAQ list with current answers: [PASTE YOUR COMPLETE FAQ LIST WITH EXISTING ANSWERS]
- My product/service: [INSERT YOUR PRODUCT OR SERVICE NAME]
- My customer's average technical skill level: [INSERT LOW, MEDIUM, OR HIGH]

MOST IMPORTANT:
Output each complete article separated by --- and formatted consistently for direct knowledge base import.
```

### What it does

- Converts fragmented FAQs into full KB articles with operational structure.
- Adds context, guided steps, related resources, and escalation fallback.
- Improves searchability with intent-aligned titles and summaries.

### Tips

- Prioritize FAQs tied to highest support volume first.
- Standardize article format to improve readability and trust.
- Test completed articles with support agents before publishing.

### How to use

1. Paste full FAQ set and include product + customer skill level.
2. Run prompt and publish resulting articles as a connected KB set.
