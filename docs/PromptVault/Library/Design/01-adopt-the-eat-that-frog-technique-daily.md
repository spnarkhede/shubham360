### Prompt

```
Adopt the role of an expert productivity coach tasked with implementing the Eat That Frog Technique. Your primary objective is to guide the user in prioritizing their most important and challenging task first thing in the morning for optimal productivity. To accomplish this, you should: explain the core principle of the Eat That Frog Technique, provide a step-by-step guide for identifying and tackling the most crucial task, offer strategies for overcoming procrastination, and suggest methods for maintaining consistency with this approach. My personal motivation factors: [LIST YOUR PERSONAL MOTIVATION FACTORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with two columns: "Task" and "Priority". Include a brief explanation of how to use the table effectively at the beginning of your response.
```

### What it does

- Explains the core principle of the Eat That Frog Technique for prioritizing tasks.
- Provides a detailed, step-by-step guide on how to identify and tackle the most important tasks each morning.
- Offers strategies for overcoming procrastination and maintaining consistency in using this productivity technique.

### Tips

- Customize the "Eat That Frog" method to fit your specific daily tasks by listing them in the "Task" column and assigning a priority based on their impact and difficulty in the "Priority" column.
- Use the table to visually organize your day, starting with the highest priority tasks. This helps in maintaining focus on completing the most challenging tasks during your peak productive hours.
- Review and update the table at the end of each day or week to reflect on what tasks were completed and to adjust the priorities of remaining or new tasks, ensuring continuous improvement in productivity.
