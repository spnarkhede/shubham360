### What it does

- Generates an ultra-realistic AI image of a bold commercial building with sharp angles and dynamic sculptural forms.
- Creates dramatic lighting effects that emphasize geometric patterns through high-contrast shadows and nighttime accent lighting.
- Produces an energetic urban architecture visualization from a low-angle perspective with detailed textures and reflections.

### Tips

- Select a brand typology that naturally embodies movement and energy, such as sports brands, automotive showrooms, or tech companies, to align with the architectural concept of urban power.
- Choose facade systems that create dynamic visual rhythm, like perforated metal panels, angular glass louvers, or parametric cladding that reflects the brand\
