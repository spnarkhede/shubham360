### What it does

- Generates an ultra-realistic AI image of a modern mixed-use building with shopping mall, office tower, and residential tower stacked vertically.

- Renders the AI image during blue hour with street-level perspective, featuring detailed materials, landscaped terraces, and realistic human activity throughout each functional zone.

### Tips

- Define your urban context by specifying the city type, climate, and street character to help the AI image create a believable setting that matches your project location.
- Customize the facade language for each program layer with specific materials like glass curtain walls for offices or textured concrete for the podium to give your AI image prompt clear visual direction.
- Use the JSON prompt to guide the AI in creating a photorealistic architectural visualization that shows the vertical layering and functional separation of your mixed-use building.
