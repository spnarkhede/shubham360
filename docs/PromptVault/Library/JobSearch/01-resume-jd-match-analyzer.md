### 1. Resume and Job Description Match Analyzer

**Use Case:** Analyze resume fit against a target job description and identify exact gaps.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Professional

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Analysis`, `ATS Optimization`

**Prompt:**

```
Analyze my resume against this job description and provide:
1) Match score (0-100%),
2) Top 5 missing keywords,
3) Experience gaps,
4) Specific phrases to incorporate.
Resume: [PASTE YOUR RESUME TEXT]
Job Description: [PASTE YOUR JOB DESCRIPTION]
```
