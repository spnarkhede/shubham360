### 3. Career Transition Resume Rewrite

**Use Case:** Reframe existing achievements for a new target role by highlighting transferable strengths.

**Target Personas:** Career Changer, Job Seeker

**Tags:** `Career Changer`, `Career Development`, `Transferable Skills`

**Prompt:**

```
I'm transitioning into a new career from [Previous Field] to [New Field]. Rewrite my resume summary and key achievements so they highlight transferable skills and make a strong case for my fit, even without direct experience.
```
