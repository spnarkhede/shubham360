### 4. Full Resume Audit

**Use Case:** Get direct feedback on weak, vague, or low-impact resume sections and improve them.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Analysis`, `Resume Audit`

**Prompt:**

```
Audit this entire resume and point out areas where I am too vague, too wordy, or not showing enough impact. Give feedback on tone, structure, and how I can better emphasize leadership, results, and innovation.
```
