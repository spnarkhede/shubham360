### 5. Interview Preparation Master

**Use Case:** Build a complete interview prep plan from one job description.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Software Engineer, Product Manager, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Interview Prep`, `Career Development`

**Prompt:**

```
Act as a Senior Interview Consultant and Hiring Manager. Using the job description below, prepare a complete interview pack with:
1) Role expectations and likely interviewer priorities,
2) Keyword and skill breakdown,
3) Beginner to advanced technical questions,
4) Behavioral interview preparation using STAR,
5) Resume-to-role mapping strategy,
6) A practical 7-day and 14-day prep schedule.

Job Description:
[PASTE THE FULL JOB DESCRIPTION HERE]
```
