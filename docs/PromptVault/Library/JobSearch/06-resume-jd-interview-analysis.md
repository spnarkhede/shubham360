### 6. Resume and JD Interview Analysis

**Use Case:** Generate personalized interview preparation by mapping resume evidence against JD requirements.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Software Engineer

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Interview Prep`, `Resume Writing`

**Prompt:**

```
Act as a Senior Interview Consultant and Technical Hiring Manager.
Analyze my resume and job description together and generate:
1) Role fit rating (Strong/Medium/Weak),
2) Keyword mapping table (JD requirement -> resume evidence -> depth),
3) Missing skills and practical mitigation strategy,
4) Likely interview challenge areas,
5) Customized technical and behavioral talking points.

Job Description:
[PASTE FULL JOB DESCRIPTION HERE]

My Resume:
[PASTE FULL RESUME HERE]
```
