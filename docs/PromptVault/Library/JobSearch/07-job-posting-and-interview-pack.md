### 1. Job Posting Keyword and Skills Scanner

**Use Case:** Extract ATS-relevant keywords, skills, action verbs, and education requirements from a job posting.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Student, Career Changer

**Tags:** `Job Search`, `ATS Optimization`, `Analysis`

**Prompt:**

```
Analyze the job or internship posting I provide and identify:
- Keywords
- Hard skill requirements and aligned action verbs
- Soft skill requirements and aligned action verbs
- Desired experience or accomplishments
- Educational background

Use the exact words and phrases from the job description and organize findings by category in order of importance.
```

### 2. AI Interview Prep Overlap Scanner

**Use Case:** Identify the overlap between a job description, resume, and cover letter to prepare for AI-screened interviews.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Student, Career Changer

**Tags:** `Interview Prep`, `Job Search`, `Analysis`

**Prompt:**

```
Assist me in identifying key words, skills, and phrases to prepare for an AI-screened interview.

Proceed in these steps:
1. Ask me for the job description.
2. Ask me for my resume and cover letter if available.
3. Identify overlaps that are common across all provided documents for:
- Keywords
- Hard skills
- Soft skills
- Action verbs
- Important experiences and accomplishments
- Educational background
4. Arrange the overlapping findings under their category headings in order of importance.
```

### 3. Interview Q and A Follow-Up Prompt

**Use Case:** Generate likely interview questions and evaluate drafted answers against job requirements.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Student, Career Changer

**Tags:** `Interview Prep`, `Behavioral Interview`, `Technical Interview`

**Prompt:**

```
Play the role of an expert career coach. Based on the specific job description provided earlier and typical roles in this field, suggest technical, behavioral, and fit interview questions I should prepare for. After I draft responses, evaluate them for alignment with the job description's keywords, skills, action verbs, experiences, and education requirements, then offer sample improved responses.
```
