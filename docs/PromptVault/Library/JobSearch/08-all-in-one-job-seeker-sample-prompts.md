### 1. Resume Match Score

**Use Case:** Compare a resume to a job posting and identify the biggest fit gaps quickly.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Professional

**Tags:** `Job Search`, `Resume Analysis`, `ATS Optimization`

**Prompt:**

```
Analyze my resume against this job posting and provide:
1. Match score (0-100%)
2. Top 5 missing keywords
3. Experience gaps
4. Specific phrases to incorporate

Format as: Score | Critical Gaps | Keywords

RESUME: [PASTE YOUR RESUME]
JOB POSTING: [PASTE JOB DESCRIPTION]
```

### 2. ATS Resume Review

**Use Case:** Identify formatting and wording issues that weaken ATS performance.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Professional

**Tags:** `Job Search`, `ATS Optimization`, `Resume Audit`

**Prompt:**

```
Review my resume for ATS optimization. Identify:
1. Formatting issues that hurt ATS scanning
2. Missing keywords from common job descriptions
3. Weak bullet points that need stronger wording
4. Action verbs to strengthen impact

MY RESUME: [PASTE RESUME]
```

### 3. Resume Bullet Rewrite with CAR Method

**Use Case:** Rewrite weak resume bullets using Challenge-Action-Result.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Professional, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `Job Search`, `Resume Writing`, `Impact Statements`

**Prompt:**

```
Rewrite these resume bullets using the CAR method and stronger power verbs:
- [BULLET 1]
- [BULLET 2]
- [BULLET 3]

Make them stronger and more quantifiable.
```

### 4. Tailored Cover Letter

**Use Case:** Generate a targeted cover letter tied to company mission and role requirements.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Professional

**Tags:** `Job Search`, `Cover Letter`, `Writing`

**Prompt:**

```
Write a compelling cover letter for this job.
Make it:
- Specific to company mission and values
- Highlight relevant skills from my background
- Show enthusiasm for the role
- 3 to 4 paragraphs, professional tone

JOB DESCRIPTION: [PASTE]
MY BACKGROUND: [DESCRIBE YOUR EXPERIENCE]
COMPANY: [COMPANY NAME]
ROLE: [POSITION TITLE]
```

### 5. Interview Question Practice

**Use Case:** Practice mapped interview questions with feedback after each answer.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Professional

**Tags:** `Interview Prep`, `Mock Interview`, `Job Search`

**Prompt:**

```
I am interviewing for [JOB TITLE] at [COMPANY].
You are the hiring manager for this role.
Job description: [PASTE DESCRIPTION]

Ask me 8 interview questions mapped to the top required skills.
After each answer:
1. Provide STAR-method feedback
2. Suggest a stronger version
3. Rate my answer from 1 to 10
```

### 6. STAR Answer Coach

**Use Case:** Turn a rough experience into a clear STAR-method answer.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Student, Professional

**Tags:** `Interview Prep`, `STAR Method`, `Job Search`

**Prompt:**

```
Help me answer this interview question using the STAR method.

Question: [PASTE QUESTION]
My experience: [DESCRIBE YOUR BACKGROUND]

Guide me through:
- Situation
- Task
- Action
- Result
```

### 7. Broaden Job Titles

**Use Case:** Expand job-title options based on existing skills and preferences.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Professional

**Tags:** `Career Exploration`, `Job Search`, `Career Planning`

**Prompt:**

```
I am seeking a new job and want to broaden the job titles I should consider.
My resume: [PASTE YOUR RESUME]

1. Analyze my skills and experience
2. Ask 3 to 4 questions about which skills I enjoy
3. Create a table of 15 or more job titles that match my profile
4. Explain why each title fits
```

### 8. Boolean Search String Builder

**Use Case:** Build a recruiter-style search string to find matching roles or candidate patterns.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Recruiter, Career Changer

**Tags:** `Job Search`, `Boolean Search`, `Research`

**Prompt:**

```
Create a Boolean search string for [JOB BOARD] to find candidates like me.

Desired role: [YOUR TARGET ROLE]
Key skills: [LIST 5-10 SKILLS]
Location: [LOCATION]
Experience: [YEARS]
Education: [DEGREE]

Create a search string with include terms, exclude terms, and location parameters.
```

### 9. Skills Gap Analysis

**Use Case:** Identify missing skills for a target role and build a short learning plan.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Student

**Tags:** `Job Search`, `Skills Gap`, `Career Planning`

**Prompt:**

```
Analyze the skills gap for my target role.

Target role: [JOB TITLE]
Current skills: [LIST YOUR SKILLS]

Provide:
1. Skills I have
2. Critical missing skills
3. Nice-to-have skills
4. A 90-day learning plan to close the gap
```
