### 2. LinkedIn Profile Summary and Personal Brand

**Use Case:** Rewrite the About section into a narrative that improves clarity, trust, and conversion.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Consultant, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Personal Branding`, `Writing`

**Prompt:**

```
Rewrite my LinkedIn About section as a three-paragraph narrative:
1) Hook that summarizes my journey in [industry],
2) 2-3 key skills plus one measurable achievement,
3) Mission, unique value, and CTA for hiring managers or clients.
Keep tone conversational and keyword-rich.
```
