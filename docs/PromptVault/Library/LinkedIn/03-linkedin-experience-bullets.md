### 3. LinkedIn Experience Rewrite with Achievement Bullets

**Use Case:** Transform role descriptions into outcome-focused bullets with metrics.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Professional, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Career Growth`, `Resume Writing`

**Prompt:**

```
Here is my current job description: [copy-paste].
Produce 5 achievement-focused bullet points for my LinkedIn Experience section.
Each bullet should begin with a strong action verb, include a metric where possible, and integrate relevant skills and industry keywords.
```
