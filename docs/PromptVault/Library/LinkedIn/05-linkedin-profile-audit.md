### 5. Full LinkedIn Profile Audit

**Use Case:** Get structured feedback on strengths, weak spots, and immediate profile fixes.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Founder, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Profile Audit`, `Career Growth`

**Prompt:**

```
You are a LinkedIn Personal Brand Consultant.
I will paste my full profile (headline, about, experience, skills).
Provide:
1) Strengths,
2) Weak spots,
3) Action plan with 5 immediate edits,
4) 3 rewritten headline options with short explanations.
```
