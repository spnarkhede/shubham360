### 6. LinkedIn Post with Hook and Actionable Insights

**Use Case:** Draft a high-engagement post with a strong hook, short story, takeaways, and CTA.

**Target Personas:** Marketer, Founder, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Content Strategy`, `Thought Leadership`

**Prompt:**

```
Write a LinkedIn post (200-250 words) for my target audience [audience] that:
1) Starts with a strong one-line hook,
2) Shares a personal story or learning related to [topic],
3) Gives 3 actionable takeaways,
4) Ends with a question inviting comments.
Tone: conversational with light wit.
```
