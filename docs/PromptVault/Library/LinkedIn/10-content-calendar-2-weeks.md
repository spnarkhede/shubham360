### 10. Two-Week LinkedIn Content Calendar

**Use Case:** Build a practical posting cadence with balanced content and engagement actions.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Consultant, Creator

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Planning`, `Content Strategy`

**Prompt:**

```
Create a 2-week LinkedIn content plan for [industry/topic].
For each week, include:
- 2 post ideas (topic + hook + format),
- 1 engagement action (commenting or poll).
Keep it strategic and achievable.
```
