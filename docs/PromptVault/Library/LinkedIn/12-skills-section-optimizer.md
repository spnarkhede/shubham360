### 12. Skills Section Optimizer

**Use Case:** Re-rank LinkedIn skills and identify missing skills that improve recruiter visibility.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Professional, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Skills`, `Keyword Strategy`

**Prompt:**

```
Here is my current Skills list: [copy-paste]. Recommend a revised top 10 skills ranking based on impact and recruiter search terms in [industry/job title]. Then suggest 5 emerging skills I should add, why they matter, and how I can demonstrate them in my profile and posts.
```
