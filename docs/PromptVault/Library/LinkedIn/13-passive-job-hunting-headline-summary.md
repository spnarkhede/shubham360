### 13. Passive Job-Hunting Headline and Summary

**Use Case:** Signal openness to opportunities without sounding actively desperate.

**Target Personas:** Professional, Job Seeker, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Recruiter Visibility`, `Personal Branding`

**Prompt:**

```
I am not actively job-hunting right now, but I am open to opportunities. Write a LinkedIn headline and summary of 120 to 150 words that improve recruiter visibility, show my expertise, and subtly signal openness to new roles. Include two meaningful metrics and one personal insight about my professional journey.
```
