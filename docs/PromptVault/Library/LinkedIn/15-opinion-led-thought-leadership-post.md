### 15. Opinion-Led Thought Leadership Post

**Use Case:** Write a clear opinion piece that balances conviction with practical advice.

**Target Personas:** Founder, Manager, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Thought Leadership`, `Writing`

**Prompt:**

```
Write a 250-word LinkedIn post sharing my opinion on [industry topic]. Start with a bold statement, explain why it matters, share one counter-point, and end with two practical tips and a CTA.
```
