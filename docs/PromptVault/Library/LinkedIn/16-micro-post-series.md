### 16. Micro-Post Series

**Use Case:** Generate a batch of short LinkedIn post ideas to maintain posting consistency.

**Target Personas:** Creator, Consultant, Job Seeker

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Content Strategy`, `Writing`

**Prompt:**

```
Generate 10 micro-post ideas, each 1 to 2 sentences, around [specific topic]. Each should include a hook, a takeaway, and one relevant hashtag.
```
