### 19. Refresh an Old Post

**Use Case:** Update an older LinkedIn post with a stronger hook and clearer reflection.

**Target Personas:** Creator, Consultant, Professional

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Content Refresh`, `Writing`

**Prompt:**

```
Here is my old LinkedIn post: [paste]. Rewrite it in a fresh format that is shorter than 180 words, uses a new hook, shifts the tone to reflective, and suggests one stronger CTA.
```
