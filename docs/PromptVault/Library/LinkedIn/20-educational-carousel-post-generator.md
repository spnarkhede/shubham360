### 20. Educational Carousel Post Generator

**Use Case:** Turn one topic into a structured LinkedIn carousel with a clear flow.

**Target Personas:** Creator, Educator, Consultant

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Carousel`, `Content Strategy`

**Prompt:**

```
Create a 7-slide LinkedIn carousel post on [topic]. Slide 1 should be the hook and title. Slides 2 to 6 should each explain one key point in 30 words or fewer. Slide 7 should provide an actionable next step and CTA. Include exact text for each slide and one visual idea for each.
```
