### 21. Short-Form Video Script

**Use Case:** Create a concise LinkedIn video script with a strong hook and CTA.

**Target Personas:** Creator, Consultant, Founder

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Video`, `Content Strategy`

**Prompt:**

```
Write a 45 to 60 second LinkedIn video script for [topic]. Use this structure:
1) 5-second hook,
2) 35 to 45 seconds of three short segments with example and tip,
3) 5 to 10 second CTA inviting comments or follows.
Include scene notes and a caption under 100 characters.
```
