### 23. Blog to Video Script

**Use Case:** Convert a blog article into a short LinkedIn video summary.

**Target Personas:** Creator, Marketer, Consultant

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Video`, `Content Repurposing`

**Prompt:**

```
Here is a blog article on [topic]: [paste link or excerpt]. Generate a concise video script of about 60 seconds summarizing the article's 3 main insights. Tone: conversational-authoritative.
```
