### 27. Connection Request with Personalized Hook

**Use Case:** Write a connection request that shows research, relevance, and a reason to respond.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Professional, Consultant

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Networking`, `Outreach`

**Prompt:**

```
Write a LinkedIn connection request message of about 90 words to [Name] at [Company]. Mention a recent post, comment, or company update that shows we share an interest in [specific topic]. Then state why I would like to connect and offer something of value, such as a tip or resource. Tone: friendly, professional, and brief.
```
