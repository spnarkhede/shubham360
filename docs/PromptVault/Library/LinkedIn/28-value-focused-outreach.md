### 28. Value-Focused Outreach

**Use Case:** Follow up after a connection accepts by sharing value instead of making a cold ask.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Consultant, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Networking`, `Outreach`

**Prompt:**

```
Write a 100-word LinkedIn message to a new contact who accepted my invite. Thank them for connecting, reference one piece of their content or their role in [industry], then share a relevant insight or question about [topic] and suggest a low-commitment next step, such as a 10-minute chat next week. Tone: conversational, no sales pitch.
```
