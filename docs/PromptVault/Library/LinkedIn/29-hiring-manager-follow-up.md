### 29. Hiring Manager Follow-Up

**Use Case:** Message a hiring manager about a role by leading with relevant achievements and curiosity about the job.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Professional

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Job Search`, `Outreach`

**Prompt:**

```
Write a 110-word LinkedIn message to a hiring manager at [Company]. Reference their job posting for [Role], highlight 2 to 3 relevant achievements of mine with metrics that match the description, and ask what they consider the top priority for the role. Tone: focused on how I can solve their problem, not just that I am applying.
```
