### 30. No-Response Follow-Up

**Use Case:** Send a polite follow-up after a message has gone unanswered for several days.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Professional, Consultant

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Follow-Up`, `Outreach`

**Prompt:**

```
Create a polite and concise follow-up message of 80 words or less to someone who has not replied in 5 to 7 days. Reference my last message, add one fresh detail about my availability or skills, ask a simple question such as whether this is still something they are exploring, and sign off professionally.
```
