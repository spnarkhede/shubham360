### 32. Client Outreach Message

**Use Case:** Write a decision-maker outreach message that leads with a likely business problem and a relevant outcome.

**Target Personas:** Freelancer, Consultant, Founder

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Business Development`, `Outreach`

**Prompt:**

```
Write a 100-word LinkedIn message to a decision-maker at [Company] in [industry]. Reference a specific challenge their company may be facing based on public information, briefly mention how I have helped similar businesses achieve [metric or benefit], and ask if they would be open to a 15-minute chat to explore solutions. Tone: confident, value-driven, not pushy.
```
