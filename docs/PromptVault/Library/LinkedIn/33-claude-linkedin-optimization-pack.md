### 1. Brutal LinkedIn Review

**Use Case:** Audit a LinkedIn profile with clear scoring, fast fixes, and a stronger headline suggestion.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Consultant, Founder

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Profile Audit`, `Personal Branding`

**Prompt:**

```
Audit this LinkedIn profile: [URL]. Score it from 1 to 10 on headline, About, experience results, Featured, keywords, and branding. List 3 mistakes, 5 quick fixes, and suggest 1 better headline. Keep it concise.
```

### 2. Headline That Earns the Click

**Use Case:** Generate multiple headline options that are keyword-aware and psychologically distinct.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Consultant, Founder

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Headline Writing`, `Search Visibility`

**Prompt:**

```
Write 10 LinkedIn headline variations for me.
My audience: [who]
The outcome I create: [result]
My unfair advantage: [why me]

Constraints:
- Under 230 characters
- Include 3 to 4 keywords my audience actually searches
- Lead with their outcome, not my title
- Avoid clichÃ©s like passionate, helping, and results-driven

For each headline, tag the angle used: clarity, curiosity, specificity, or proof.
```

### 3. About Section That Converts

**Use Case:** Rewrite the About section as conversion-focused profile copy rather than a resume summary.

**Target Personas:** Consultant, Founder, Service Provider

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `About Section`, `Conversion Copy`

**Prompt:**

```
Rewrite my LinkedIn About section as conversion copy, not a resume.

Structure:
1. A hook naming my reader's top frustration
2. A turning-point moment that earned my point of view
3. The outcomes I deliver with specific numbers
4. Who I work with and who I do not
5. A clear CTA

Use first person, short lines, and stay under 2,000 characters.

Context: [paste current About + 3 client wins + my ICP]
```

### 4. Positioning Pressure Test

**Use Case:** Tighten a broad LinkedIn position by diagnosing audience, pain point, and proof.

**Target Personas:** Consultant, Founder, Personal Brand Builder

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Positioning`, `Personal Branding`

**Prompt:**

```
Based on my LinkedIn profile: [URL], my positioning feels too broad.

Ask me 10 diagnostic questions, one at a time, to pinpoint:
- My sharpest ICP
- The exact problem I solve better than most
- The proof I can defend

After my answers, output:
1. A one-sentence positioning statement
2. 3 signature talking points
3. The actual words my ICP uses to describe their pain so I can mirror them in my profile
```

### 5. Featured Section Conversion Strategy

**Use Case:** Redesign a Featured section to turn profile views into more DMs and clicks.

**Target Personas:** Consultant, Founder, Freelancer

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Featured Section`, `Conversion Strategy`

**Prompt:**

```
My profile gets views but very few DMs.

Help me redesign my Featured section for better conversion.
My target audience: [describe]
My offer: [describe]

Deliver:
1. The 4 Featured items I should showcase and the role of each: credibility, education, lead magnet, or social proof
2. The exact CTA or link text for each item
3. One simple asset I should create if something is missing
4. A rewritten closing line for my About section that drives clicks to Featured
```
