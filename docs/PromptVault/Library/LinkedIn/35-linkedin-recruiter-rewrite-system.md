### 1. Brutal Audit for Target Job Descriptions

**Use Case:** Diagnose the mismatch between a LinkedIn profile and a target set of job descriptions before rewriting.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Profile Audit`, `Job Search`

**Prompt:**

```
You are my friend who has been a senior tech recruiter at [target companies] for 10 years. You owe me honesty, not politeness.

I am attaching my LinkedIn profile and 5 job descriptions I want to be competitive for.

Do not rewrite anything yet. Give me a diagnostic:
1. Identity gap
2. Keyword gap
3. Credibility gap
4. Search visibility
5. The one line my profile is saying today versus what it should say

Quote my actual profile lines back to me. Do not soften.
```

### 2. Headline and About Rewrite with Constraints

**Use Case:** Rewrite headline and About section against target JDs using strict constraints.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Headline Writing`, `About Section`

**Prompt:**

```
Based on the audit above, rewrite my headline and About section.

Headline rules:
- Max 120 characters
- Include the exact role keyword from at least 3 attached JDs
- Lead with the most credibility-dense fact I have
- Avoid: passionate, results-driven, transformational, dynamic, seasoned, strategic
- Give me 3 ranked versions with one sentence on why each works

About rules:
- Max 2,000 characters
- First line must be a hook, not "I'm a [title]"
- Paragraph 1: what I build and the scale
- Paragraph 2: 3-sentence origin story if it differentiates me
- Paragraph 3: what I am working on now and what conversations I want to have
- End with one specific CTA
- Use mobile-first formatting with short line breaks
- Give me 2 full versions
```

### 3. Experience Bullet Surgery

**Use Case:** Rewrite Experience bullets in a recruiter-readable structure with measurable evidence.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Professional, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Experience Section`, `Achievement Writing`

**Prompt:**

```
For each role in my Experience section, rewrite the bullets using this structure:
[Strong verb] + [specific scope or scale] + [measurable outcome] + [business impact]

Rules:
- 3 bullets per role max
- Every bullet must contain at least one number or use [METRIC NEEDED]
- Lead with the strongest bullet
- Use the exact vocabulary from the 5 attached JDs
- Avoid these verbs: spearheaded, championed, drove alignment, leveraged synergies, owned the vision, delivered on

For each role, show:
- Current bullets
- Rewritten bullets
- A list of [METRIC NEEDED] gaps
```

### 4. Featured Section and Skills Strategy

**Use Case:** Build a recruiter-scannable Featured section and a stronger skills ranking strategy.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Professional, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Featured Section`, `Skills Strategy`

**Prompt:**

```
Based on my target JDs and my rewritten profile, design my Featured section and Skills section.

Featured:
- 4 slots max
- What goes in each slot and in what order
- If I do not have an asset yet, suggest the specific asset to create
- Give a one-line mobile-friendly description for each slot

Skills:
- Top 10 skills to pin ranked by JD relevance
- Identify the best 3 Top Skills
- Call out any skill that appears in 3 or more JDs but is missing from my profile
- Suggest 5 people from my network I should ask for endorsements and the exact 2-line DM to send
```

### 5. Four-Week Authority Stack

**Use Case:** Build a posting plan that supports a stronger LinkedIn profile before outreach.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Builder, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Content Strategy`, `Authority Building`

**Prompt:**

```
Based on my new positioning, generate a 4-week LinkedIn content plan to build authority before I start cold outreach.

Structure:
- Week 1: 3 builder credibility posts
- Week 2: 3 lessons-from-current-role posts
- Week 3: 3 POV posts on my domain
- Week 4: 3 behind-the-scenes posts

For each of the 12 posts, give me:
- Hook
- Body angle
- Format
- One specific question to end with

Sequence them so they compound into a coherent recruiter narrative.
```
