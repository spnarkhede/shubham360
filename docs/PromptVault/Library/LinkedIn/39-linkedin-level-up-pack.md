### 1. Strong Headline Prompt

**Use Case:** Create a concise headline anchored to expertise, skills, and career goal.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Professional, Career Changer

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Headline Writing`, `Career Growth`

**Prompt:**

```
Create a LinkedIn headline that highlights my expertise in [field], key skills like [skills], and goal to [career objective].
```

---

### 2. Optimized About Section Prompt

**Use Case:** Write a confident, clear, and career-focused About section.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Professional, Career Changer

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `About Section`, `Profile Optimization`

**Prompt:**

```
Write a professional and engaging About section for LinkedIn. I am a [role] with experience in [industries or skills]. I want to sound confident, clear, and career-focused.
```

---

### 3. Role-Based Summary Prompt

**Use Case:** Condense one role into short, measurable bullet points.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Professional, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Experience Section`, `Achievement Writing`

**Prompt:**

```
Summarize my experience as a [Job Title] at [Company Name] in 3 to 4 bullet points using action verbs and measurable results.
```

### 4. SEO-Optimized Profile Prompt

**Use Case:** Identify the top recruiter keywords and place them naturally in the profile.

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Professional, Career Changer

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Keywords`, `Search Visibility`

**Prompt:**

What are the top 10 keywords recruiters search for in [industry]? Help me naturally include these in my LinkedIn profile.
