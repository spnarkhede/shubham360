### 1. Profile Audit for Personal Brand Clarity

**Use Case:** Diagnose whether a profile clearly communicates audience, value, and positioning.

**Target Personas:** Creator, Founder, Consultant

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Profile Audit`, `Personal Branding`

**Prompt:**

```
Here is my LinkedIn profile. Give me detailed feedback on:
1. Who I appear to serve
2. Whether my value is clear in the headline and About section
3. What a confused visitor might think I do
4. Three ways I can tighten my positioning without sounding salesy or robotic
```

### 2. Content Calendar Creation for Personal Brand

**Use Case:** Build a 4-week content calendar without outsourcing voice or writing style.

**Target Personas:** Creator, Founder, Service Provider

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Content Calendar`, `Personal Branding`

**Prompt:**

```
I want a 4-week LinkedIn content calendar for a [creator, founder, service provider, or job seeker] building a personal brand. Each week should include 2 emotional or story-driven posts, 1 authority post, and 1 audience engagement post. Add prompts, not finished posts. I want to write in my own voice.
```

### 4. Thought Structure for Posts

**Use Case:** Turn a messy idea into a stronger post outline without writing the post itself.

**Target Personas:** Creator, Founder, Consultant

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Post Strategy`, `Writing Support`

**Prompt:**

```
Here is a messy brain dump for a LinkedIn post. Help me turn it into a structured outline: hook, context, insight, relatable example, CTA. Do not write the post. Just make my point clearer and tighter.
```

### 5. Content Performance Analysis Prompt

**Use Case:** Review several past posts to extract repeatable strengths and weaknesses.

**Target Personas:** Creator, Founder, Consultant

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Content Analysis`, `Personal Branding`

**Prompt:**

```
Here are 5 LinkedIn posts I have written. Analyze each one and tell me:
1. Which had the strongest hook
2. Which built the most credibility
3. Which lacked emotional resonance
4. How I can combine the strongest parts in future posts
```

### 6. Personal Story Reframing Prompt

**Use Case:** Find the universal insight and useful lesson inside a personal story.

**Target Personas:** Creator, Founder, Consultant

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Storytelling`, `Personal Branding`

**Prompt:**

```
Here is a personal story I want to tell, but I am not sure how to make it valuable for others. Help me find the universal insight, a business or life lesson angle, and one way to tie it back to what I do without making it a pitch.
```

### 7. Lead Magnet Development Prompt

**Target Personas:** Creator, Founder, Consultant

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Lead Magnet`, `Personal Branding`

**Prompt:**


### 8. Positioning Course-Correction Prompt

**Use Case:** Diagnose when recent content is attracting the wrong audience and fix it carefully.

**Target Personas:** Creator, Founder, Consultant

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Positioning`, `Audience Strategy`

**Prompt:**

```
I feel like my content is attracting the wrong kind of audience. Based on this list of recent posts and offers, analyze who I am really speaking to, what my positioning implies about me, and how I can course-correct subtly without confusing people who already follow me.
```
