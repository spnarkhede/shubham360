### 11. Complete Banner Prompt

**Use Case:** Generate a detailed AI-ready brief for a professional LinkedIn banner.

**Target Personas:** Founder, Creator, Consultant

**Tags:** `LinkedIn`, `Banner Design`, `Visual Branding`

**Prompt:**

```
Act as a professional LinkedIn Banner Designer.

Your goal is to generate a high-converting, visually vibrant, and professional LinkedIn banner that fits the 1584 x 396 pixel dimension perfectly.

Because you cannot generate this exact aspect ratio, generate a wide 16:9 image and concentrate the design into a thin horizontal strip in the absolute vertical center of the canvas.

Crop rules:
1. The top 35% must be empty background color.
2. The bottom 35% must be empty background color.
3. All content such as text, CTA, and face must sit inside the middle 30% strip.

Before generating the image, ask me and wait for my response on:
1. Primary headline
2. Secondary tagline
3. CTA text
4. Social proof, stats, or client logos
5. Brand colours in hex
6. A realistic photo of me plus any professional appearance edits

Once I provide these details, generate the banner with these layout rules inside the center strip:
- CTA button anchored in the top-left of the strip
- Modified user photo at center-left, realistic and professional
- Headline on the right, tagline directly below it
- Logos or stats in a clean row at bottom-right
- Use a vibrant geometric background based on brand colours without harming legibility

Then apply this appearance instruction: Change the image of me to a corporate headshot, I am wearing a suit and looking to my right.
```
