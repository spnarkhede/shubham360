### What it does

- Generates a 2×3 grid AI image advertisement showcasing a bread product across six distinct panels with premium food photography styling.

### Tips

- Choose a specific food product that fits the everyday premium category, like artisan sourdough or multigrain rolls, and select a color palette that reflects your brand personality, such as warm earth tones for wholesome appeal or cool minimalist grays for modern sophistication.
- Use the JSON prompt to guide the AI in creating each of the six distinct panels, ensuring your product name appears consistently across the packaging shot and maintains visual harmony with the Scandinavian kitchen aesthetic.
- Consider filling the color palette variable with 2-3 complementary shades that enhance the natural daylight mood, such as soft cream, warm oak brown, and muted sage green for an organic feel.
