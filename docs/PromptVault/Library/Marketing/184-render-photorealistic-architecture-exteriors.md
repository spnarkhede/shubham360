### What it does

- Generates a photorealistic AI image of a 5-7 story contemporary mixed-use building in an urban setting.
- Creates authentic architectural visualization with natural materials, balanced proportions, and street-level perspective.
- Produces high-resolution exterior architecture renders suitable for competitions, marketing, and professional presentations.

### Tips

- Choose urban character details that match your project location, such as historic downtown for brick context or modern district for glass surroundings, to make the AI image feel authentic.
- Define facade identity by selecting primary materials like textured concrete or warm timber cladding, paired with secondary elements such as metal panels or stone accents that reflect your design vision.
- Specify street program uses like cafes, retail shops, or co-working lobbies to help the AI image prompt generate realistic ground floor activity that matches your mixed-use building goals.
