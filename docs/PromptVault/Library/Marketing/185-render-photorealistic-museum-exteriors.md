### What it does

- Generates a highly photorealistic AI image of a contemporary museum that wraps a historic building with a modern glass envelope.
- Creates architectural contrast by layering transparent glass facades around preserved heritage structures in a symmetrical composition.
- Produces professional architectural photography showing the museum as a cultural landmark with plaza foreground and eye-level perspective.

### Tips

- Select heritage materials that reflect your project location, such as limestone for European contexts or brick for industrial sites, and choose glass character like clear for transparency or lightly tinted for subtle warmth.
- Define your heritage context by specifying the surrounding urban fabric, whether a historic city center with cobblestone streets or a cultural district with period buildings, to ground the AI image in realistic site conditions.
- Use the JSON prompt to guide the AI in creating architectural photography that balances the historic facade features like arched windows or ornamental details with contemporary glass envelope design.
