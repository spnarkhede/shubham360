### What it does

- Generates a high-end beauty product AI image with a female model holding the product at eye level in a tight portrait composition.
- Creates hyperrealistic skin texture with 8K detail while maintaining natural beauty through soft diffused lighting and shallow depth of field.
- Produces a luxury skincare campaign aesthetic where the product label remains clearly visible and naturally integrated with the model\

### Tips

- Upload your product image in high-resolution on transparent background to ensure the label remains sharp and readable when positioned over the model\
