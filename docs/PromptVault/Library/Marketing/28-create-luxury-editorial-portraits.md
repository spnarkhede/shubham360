> Generate luxury editorial portraits with this AI prompt, featuring elegant subjects, animal companions, dramatic jungle lighting, and high-fashion cinematic composition.

### Prompt

```
luxury editorial portrait of an elegant [SUBJECT IDENTITY] standing beside a [ANIMAL COMPANION] in [ENVIRONMENT SETTING], cinematic fashion photography subject (woman): [FULL SUBJECT DESCRIPTION] clothing and jewelry: [WARDROBE AND ADORNMENT] prop (weapon): [CEREMONIAL PROP DESCRIPTION] animal: [ANIMAL DESCRIPTION] environment: [ENVIRONMENT DETAIL] lighting: dramatic natural sunlight cutting through the jungle canopy, warm highlights on the woman's skin and gold jewelry, deep emerald shadows in the background creating contrast color palette: rich jungle greens, warm golden skin tones, metallic gold highlights, earthy browns of the leopard, deep teal and emerald foliage composition: woman positioned slightly left of center with the spear rising vertically beside her, leopard positioned to the right with its head level with the woman's shoulder, dense foliage framing both subjects style: high fashion editorial photography, exotic jungle aesthetic, cinematic lighting, luxury magazine cover style camera: 85mm lens, shallow depth of field, sharp focus on woman and leopard, soft blurred foliage behind ultra detailed, cinematic lighting, high fashion editorial, natural textures, vibrant colors --chaos 30 --ar 3:4 --sref 3775199334 1360520854 3015451063 --p
```

### What it does

- Creates a luxury editorial AI image featuring an elegant subject posed with an animal companion in a lush jungle setting.
- Uses dramatic natural lighting and rich color tones to highlight fashion elements like gold jewelry against emerald foliage.
- Generates a cinematic magazine-style portrait with shallow depth of field that keeps the subject and animal in sharp focus.

### Tips

- Select a subject identity and animal companion that create visual harmony, such as pairing a warrior queen with a leopard or a tribal priestess with a python for your AI image prompt.
- Describe wardrobe details that complement the environment setting, like gold-threaded silk robes for jungle scenes or leather adorned with feathers for savanna backdrops.
- Choose ceremonial props and animal descriptions that tell a cohesive story, matching ornate spears with regal big cats or carved staffs with exotic birds to enhance the luxury editorial feel of your AI image.
