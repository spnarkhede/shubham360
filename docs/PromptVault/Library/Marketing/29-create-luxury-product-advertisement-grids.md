### What it does

- Generates a luxury 2×2 grid AI image showcasing a premium pet bed across four distinct panels in square format.
- Creates professional lifestyle scenes that blend product photography with interior design aesthetics using soft natural lighting.
- Produces high-end marketing visuals featuring hero shots, comfort moments, material close-ups, and styled interior integration for sophisticated pet brands.

### Tips

- Choose a product name that reflects luxury and comfort, select a product type like dog bed or cat lounger, and define a color palette with neutral tones such as ivory, taupe, or charcoal to match modern interiors.
- Consider featuring a well-groomed breed that aligns with your target audience, such as a golden retriever for warmth or a sleek greyhound for elegance, ensuring the pet appears relaxed and content in the AI image.
- Use the JSON prompt to guide the AI in creating a cohesive 2x2 grid that balances product focus with lifestyle context, ensuring each panel tells part of your brand story while maintaining visual consistency across all four images.
