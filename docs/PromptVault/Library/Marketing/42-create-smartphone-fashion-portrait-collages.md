### What it does

- Generates a 4-photo grid collage AI image showing a young woman in various fashion poses captured with smartphone photography style.
- Creates ultra-realistic portraits with natural lighting that highlight skin texture, fabric details, and candid expressions across different angles.
- Produces a minimalist modern aesthetic AI image with soft colors and airy tones perfect for fashion editorial content.

### Tips

- Choose hair descriptions that complement the outfit style, such as loose beach waves for casual looks or sleek ponytails for modern minimalist aesthetics.
- Select outfit ensembles that align with the minimalist color palette, like white crop tops with high-waisted denim or soft gray loungewear for cohesive AI image results.
- Pick background settings that enhance the airy mood, such as bright white studio spaces, urban concrete walls, or soft natural light interiors to match the smartphone fashion portrait style.
