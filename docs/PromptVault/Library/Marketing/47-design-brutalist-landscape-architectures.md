### What it does

- Generates an AI image of a brutalist landscape architecture where the ground becomes the building itself.
- Creates sunken courtyards and elevated platforms carved into natural terrain with exposed concrete and native vegetation.
- Produces an ultra-realistic aerial view showing people moving through monumental spaces integrated with rocks and trees.

### Tips

- Choose a site landscape that complements your design vision, such as desert plateaus for stark geometry or forested hillsides for organic integration with existing trees.
- Select ground geometry that defines your spatial character, like angular brutalist planes for dramatic voids or flowing curved surfaces for gentle terrain transitions.
- Specify time of day based on shadow needs, using midday for strong geometric contrast or golden hour for softer atmospheric depth in your AI image prompt.
