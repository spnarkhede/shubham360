### What it does

- Generates an AI image of a modern wellness spa interior with sculptural zones and curved pathways instead of traditional rooms.
- Creates a cool, contemporary atmosphere using color-blocked architectural surfaces, organic furniture forms, and soft ambient lighting.
- Produces ultra-realistic architectural visualization showcasing tactile materials, editorial composition, and artistic spatial design perfect for luxury spa branding.

### Tips

- Choose a bold material palette like terrazzo, brushed brass, or textured plaster to fill the primary materials variable, and pair it with secondary materials such as linen, smoked glass, or natural oak for contrast.
- Define your color world by selecting a cohesive base like soft terracotta, sage green, or warm sand tones, then add accent colors such as deep rust, charcoal, or dusty rose to create color-blocked architectural surfaces.
- Fill the sensory elements variable with creative water features like a shallow reflecting pool with pebbles, a minimalist rain curtain, or a sculptural stone fountain to enhance the immersive spa experience.
