### What it does

- Generates a luxury 2×2 grid AI image showcasing a bathroom product across four distinct panels with architectural and lifestyle photography styles.
- Creates detailed scenes including a hero shot, water flow close-up, spa ritual moment, and material precision view for premium advertising.
- Produces high-realism AI images with soft natural lighting, minimalist composition, and refined mood for luxury home lifestyle marketing.

### Tips

- Choose a specific luxury bathroom fixture like rainfall showerhead, freestanding tub faucet, or smart shower system, then select complementary metal finishes such as brushed gold with matte black trim or polished chrome with brass accents to match your brand aesthetic.
- Define your architectural environment with precise details like minimalist marble spa bathroom with floor-to-ceiling windows or Japanese-inspired stone sanctuary with bamboo accents to create a cohesive luxury setting across all four panels.
- Use the JSON prompt to guide the AI in creating a sophisticated 2x2 grid advertisement, selecting a refined color palette like warm neutrals with champagne metallics or cool grays with platinum accents that reinforces your product\
