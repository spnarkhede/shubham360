### What it does

- Generates a 3-panel vertical AI image showcasing a luxury leather accessory through different photography angles.
- Creates hero product shots, detailed close-ups of craftsmanship, and lifestyle scenes in editorial fashion style.
- Produces a minimalist fashion advertisement with sophisticated lighting and elegant typography for premium brand marketing.

### Tips

- Choose an accessory type that matches your brand identity, such as handbags for elegance, watches for precision, or sunglasses for lifestyle appeal.
- Select a color palette that complements the deep espresso brown leather, using neutral tones like cream and beige or contrasting shades like charcoal and gold for visual impact.
- Use the JSON prompt to guide the AI in creating a three-panel fashion advertisement that showcases your premium accessory from multiple angles.
