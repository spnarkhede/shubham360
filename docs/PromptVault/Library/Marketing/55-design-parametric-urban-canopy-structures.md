### What it does

- Generates a photorealistic AI image of a flowing pedestrian canopy with organic parametric design in an urban setting.
- Creates integrated seating and shaded walkways that weave around existing trees without removing them.
- Produces an eye-level architectural visualization showing people interacting with the human-scale structure in natural daylight.

### Tips

- Choose an urban setting that matches your project context, such as downtown plaza, waterfront promenade, or neighborhood street, to ground the AI image in a realistic location.
- Select canopy identity materials like white powder-coated steel, natural timber slats, or recycled aluminum mesh that reflect your sustainability goals and design aesthetic.
- Define ground plane materials such as permeable concrete pavers, natural stone, or recycled rubber surfacing to complement the canopy structure and enhance the pedestrian experience.
