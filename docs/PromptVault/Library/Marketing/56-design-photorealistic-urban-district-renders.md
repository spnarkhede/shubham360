### What it does

- Generates a highly photorealistic AI image of a buildable urban cultural district with realistic architecture and public spaces.
- Places everyday people in natural poses throughout plazas, promenades, and courtyards to show authentic human-centered activity.
- Uses architectural photography style with human eye-level perspective to capture realistic materials, natural lighting, and subtle public art integration.

### Tips

- Choose a specific regional context that reflects your project location, such as Mediterranean coastal city or Nordic urban center, to guide material and climate choices in the AI image prompt.
- Define your cultural program by selecting concrete building types like contemporary art museum, community theater, or artisan workshops, and pair them with matching art typologies such as bronze sculptures or interactive light installations.
- Specify material reality through buildable combinations like exposed concrete with timber accents or local stone with steel frames, then derive your color palette from these materials for authentic urban tones in your AI image.
