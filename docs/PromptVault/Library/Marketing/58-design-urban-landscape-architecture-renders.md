### What it does

- Generates a photorealistic AI image of a contemporary urban landscape designed as spatial infrastructure with carved terrain and architectural elements.
- Creates human eye-level views showing concrete platforms, stone paving, controlled native planting, and shallow water features within a city context.
- Produces an AI image prompt that emphasizes material texture, natural human activity, and buildable landscape architecture rather than decorative park design.

### Tips

- Fill in the landscape program variable with specific uses like urban plaza, pocket park, or transit hub greenspace to shape the AI image design direction.
- Choose your city context carefully to influence architectural backdrop and urban density, such as dense European core, North American downtown, or Asian megacity fabric.
- Select a light condition that matches your presentation goal, like soft morning light for calm atmosphere, harsh midday sun for dramatic shadows, or golden hour for warmth.
