> Generate authentic sports lifestyle photos with this AI prompt, featuring natural poses, golden hour lighting, smartphone aesthetics, and urban athletic environments.

### Prompt

```
Smartphone photo of [SUBJECT DESCRIPTION], standing at the edge of [SPORTS ENVIRONMENT], [OUTFIT DESCRIPTION], casual confident pose with one hand in pocket, natural candid expression, athletic stadium lights and chain-link fence in background, urban park setting, slightly messy wind-blown blonde hair, glowing golden hour light, realistic skin texture, subtle natural makeup, vibrant yet natural colors, slight motion blur from players in background, shallow depth of field from smartphone portrait mode, iPhone 15 Pro camera look, HDR, soft lens flare, casual Instagram aesthetic, authentic street-sports vibe, high detail, realistic proportions
```

### What it does

- Generates a smartphone-style AI image of a subject posed at a sports location during golden hour.
- Captures casual confidence with natural lighting, realistic textures, and iPhone portrait mode effects.
- Creates an authentic Instagram aesthetic with background motion blur and urban sports atmosphere.

### Tips

- Choose a specific sport and environment that matches your content goals, like a soccer player at a turf field or a basketball enthusiast at an outdoor court with graffiti walls.
- Describe outfit details that reflect the sport and personal style, such as a vintage team jersey with ripped jeans or athletic joggers with a cropped hoodie for authentic street-sports appeal.
- Adjust the subject description to include distinctive features like tattoos, accessories, or body language that adds personality to your AI image prompt and makes it feel more genuine for social media.
