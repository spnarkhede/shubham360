> Generate stunning nighttime street portraits with this AI prompt, featuring natural lighting, flash photography aesthetics, and customizable subject styling.

### Prompt

```
A candid nighttime street portrait of [SUBJECT DESCRIPTION]. She has long black hair, natural makeup with a soft glow, and wears [CASUAL WARDROBE]. She leans slightly to the side with one arm resting on the chair, looking off-camera with a calm, introspective expression. Subtle accessories include thin gold bracelets and a delicate necklace. The scene is lit with a direct flash, creating sharp highlights on her skin and a contrast against the dark street background. [NIGHT ENVIRONMENT]. Medium shot, shallow depth of field, film-like grain, flash photography aesthetic, raw and unfiltered mood, stylish yet natural composition --ar 3:4 --raw
```

### What it does

- Generates a candid AI image of a subject with natural makeup in casual clothing during nighttime street photography.
- Uses direct flash lighting to create sharp highlights and strong contrast against dark urban backgrounds.
- Produces a film-like aesthetic with shallow depth of field and raw, unfiltered mood in a 3:4 aspect ratio.

### Tips

- Choose a subject description that matches your creative vision, such as a young woman in her twenties or a confident professional, to set the tone for your AI image prompt.
- Select casual wardrobe options like an oversized denim jacket, a simple white tee, or a vintage leather coat to create an authentic street style look that feels natural and relatable.
- Define your night environment with specific details such as neon-lit alleyway, quiet urban sidewalk with streetlights, or bustling city corner with blurred traffic to add depth and atmosphere to your AI image.
