> Generate cinematic office photography with this AI prompt, featuring golden hour lighting, professional subjects, high-rise settings, and inspiring success-focused compositions.

### Prompt

```
{
  "prompt_description": "A cinematic, professionally photographed office scene featuring [SUBJECT DESCRIPTION], sitting at a sleek, dark marble desk. [SUBJECT PRONOUN] is wearing [WARDROBE]. The lighting is warm golden hour, pouring through tall office windows that offer a high-rise city view with buildings in the soft background. [SUBJECT PRONOUN] is lightly interacting with an open silver laptop (MacBook Pro style) that shows a graph. A dark leather notebook and a stylish golden pen are neatly placed on the desk. The background features strong depth-of-field bokeh, with large, warm, circular light reflections (or orbs of light) adding a visionary, movie-like aesthetic. The camera angle is slightly low and empowering. The overall mood is inspiring, focused, and successful.",
  "style": "Cinematic, Professional Photography, Golden Hour",
  "subject": "[SUBJECT DESCRIPTION]",
  "setting": "Modern High-Rise Office, Sleek Desk",
  "lighting": "Warm Golden Hour Light, Bokeh Background",
  "camera_angle": "Slightly low, Empowering Perspective",
  "mood_keywords": ["Inspiring", "Visionary", "Success", "Elegance", "Focus"]
}
```

### What it does

- Generates a cinematic AI image of a professional subject working at a modern office desk during golden hour.
- Uses warm lighting and bokeh effects to create an inspiring, movie-like atmosphere with depth.
- Captures an empowering low-angle view that showcases success through elegant office details and city backdrop.

### Tips

- Fill the subject description variable with specific details like age range, ethnicity, hairstyle, and expression to create an authentic professional character that resonates with your target audience.
- Choose wardrobe that matches your brand identity, such as tailored blazers for corporate elegance or smart casual attire for modern startup vibes, ensuring colors complement the warm golden hour lighting.
- Use the JSON prompt to guide the AI in creating a cinematic office scene, and consider customizing the laptop graph to show relevant business metrics like growth charts or analytics dashboards for added storytelling depth.
