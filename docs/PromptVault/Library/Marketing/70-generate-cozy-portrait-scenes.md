> Generate heartwarming photorealistic portraits with this AI prompt, featuring customizable seasonal scenes, cozy atmospheres, outfits, companions, and consistent facial features.

### Prompt

```
{
  "description": "A cute woman with rosy cheeks, sitting by a window on a [SEASON/WEATHER] day, wearing a [OUTFIT DESCRIPTION]. She is warmly hugging a large [PLUSH TOY/COMPANION] with a red scarf around its neck. A hot chocolate mug sits beside her. The lighting is warm, soft, and cozy, creating an extremely heartwarming and photorealistic atmosphere in 8k resolution. Do not change the woman's face.",
  "signature": "Shreya Yadav"
}
```

### What it does

- Generates a photorealistic AI image of a woman in a cozy indoor setting with customizable seasonal and outfit elements.
- Features warm lighting and heartwarming details like a plush companion and hot chocolate to create an inviting atmosphere.
- Produces an 8k resolution AI image while preserving the woman\

### Tips

- Choose a season or weather condition that matches the mood you want to create, such as snowy winter for maximum coziness or rainy autumn for a melancholic feel.
- Select an outfit description that complements the cozy atmosphere, like an oversized knit sweater or flannel pajamas, to enhance the warmth of your AI image prompt.
- Pick a plush toy or companion that adds personality to the scene, such as a teddy bear for classic comfort or a giant stuffed bunny for playful charm.
