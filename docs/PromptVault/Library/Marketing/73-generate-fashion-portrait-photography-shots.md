### Prompt

```
{"image_generation_prompt":{"subject":{"demographics":"[SUBJECT DESCRIPTION]","appearance":"Fair skin tone, slender and fit physique, attractive facial features","hair":"Long black hair tied up in a high, slightly messy ponytail with loose wisps framing the face and neck","makeup":"Professional fashion makeup, winged eyeliner, soft blush, reddish-pink lipstick","tattoo":"Visible tattoo(s) on her body"},"clothing":{"main_piece":"[OUTFIT]","material":"Satin or silk texture","details":"Spaghetti straps, structured bodice/corset top, backless or low back design"},"pose":{"stance":"Standing in profile, body turned slightly away from the camera","arms":"Left hand resting confidently on the hip, right arm raised with hand gently touching back of head/hair","gaze":"Looking back over her shoulder directly at the camera with a confident, alluring expression"},"setting":{"location":"[SETTING]","background":"Decorated [SETTING] background with relevant furniture, surfaces, and fixtures","props_and_clutter":"A black flight case/equipment box on the floor to the left, a black V-flat or light blocker panel, metal light stands visible in the background","floor":"[SETTING] floor, not plain white"},"lighting":{"style":"High-key studio lighting with strong backlighting","effects":"Rim light highlighting the silhouette of her arm and hair, soft diffuse fill light on the face, creating a glow effect"},"technical_details":{"style":"Fashion photography, editorial portrait, photorealistic","camera":"DSLR, 85mm lens","focus":"Sharp focus on the subject, slightly soft background","quality":"8k resolution, high definition, detailed texture"}}}
```

### What it does

- Generates a high-quality AI image of a fashion model in an elegant pose wearing a satin outfit in a studio setting.
- Uses professional studio lighting with rim light and backlight to create a glowing editorial portrait effect.
- Captures a confident over-the-shoulder look with sharp focus and photorealistic details at 8k resolution.
