> Generate stunning street-style candid photography with this AI prompt, featuring friends at cafés with natural lighting, composition details, and cinematic styling.

### Prompt

```
Street-style candid photography of two close friends enjoying [VENUE TYPE], seated at a small wooden table near a large window, warm natural morning light streaming through glass, soft golden highlights on their faces, [SUBJECT PAIR], relaxed body language, leaning slightly toward each other while laughing, expressive eyes, genuine smiles, ceramic coffee cups with latte art on the table, small plates with croissants, ambient café details in background blurred barista counter, hanging Edison bulbs, indoor plants, rustic brick walls, cinematic shallow depth of field, 35mm lens, f/2.0, soft bokeh, warm color grading, intimate candid moment, lifestyle editorial photography, natural skin texture, flattering window light, high detail, ultra-realistic --chaos 40 --ar 3:4 --exp 50 --raw --sref 1468140782 170932194 217014387 --profile ulg9vw8 nfzvcko --stylize 300
```

### What it does

- Generates a street-style AI image of two friends sharing a candid moment at a venue with natural morning light.
- Captures intimate details like latte art, croissants, and genuine expressions using shallow depth of field techniques.
- Produces ultra-realistic lifestyle photography with warm tones, soft bokeh, and editorial quality composition.

### Tips

- Choose a venue type that matches your brand story, such as cozy café for warmth, rooftop bar for energy, or bookstore for creativity, to set the right mood for your AI image.
- Select subject pairs that represent your target audience, like young professionals for corporate brands, college students for lifestyle products, or parent-child duos for family services, ensuring authentic connection in the scene.
- Adjust the chaos value between 20-60 to control creative variation, using lower numbers for consistent brand imagery or higher values for unique editorial content in your AI image prompt.
