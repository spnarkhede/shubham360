### What it does

- Generates a 2×2 grid AI image showcasing an office product through four distinct panels with professional photography style.
- Creates lifestyle scenes that demonstrate ergonomic features and smart controls in modern workspace environments.
- Produces a clean advertisement AI image with natural lighting and premium branding for professional marketing campaigns.

### Tips

- Choose a specific product name like "ErgoDesk Pro" or "FlexStand Elite" and match your workspace environment to your target audience, such as minimalist home office for remote workers or sleek corporate setting for enterprise clients.
- Define your color palette using your brand colors, like charcoal gray and brushed aluminum for tech brands or warm oak and matte white for lifestyle brands, ensuring the AI image maintains visual consistency across all four panels.
- Use the JSON prompt to guide the AI in creating a cohesive 2x2 grid advertisement, and specify your smart interface details like touch-sensitive height controls or LED display panels to showcase your product\
