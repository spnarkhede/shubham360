### Prompt

```
A young [SUBJECT DESCRIPTION] is sitting in a salon chair with a relaxed, confident pose. She has both legs crossed and resting on a small, dark wooden table in front of her. She is wearing a large, oversized white t-shirt with the word '[CUSTOM TEXT]' printed in black across the chest, ripped light blue jeans, and white high-top sneakers. She is wearing brown-tinted sunglasses, a silver chain necklace, and a black digital watch (G-Shock style) on her left wrist. Her right hand is gently touching her chin, and her left hand is resting on the armrest. Her hair is long, straight, and a soft, neutral brown tone. The setting is a brightly lit [SETTING] with warm indoor lighting, featuring salon chairs, shelves stocked with hair products, and mirrors in the background. It is a medium shot with centered composition, emphasizing a photorealistic street-style aesthetic with high detail on the clothing textures, jewelry, and subtle skin shine.
```

### What it does

- Generates a photorealistic AI image of a young person in a relaxed salon setting with customizable subject details.
- Features street-style fashion including an oversized t-shirt with custom text, ripped jeans, and accessories like sunglasses and jewelry.
- Creates a medium shot composition with high detail on clothing textures and a warm, brightly lit salon background.
