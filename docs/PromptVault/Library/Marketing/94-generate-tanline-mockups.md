### Tips

- Choose a short and memorable brand text that works well as a tanline on skin, such as a brand name, slogan, or website URL that fits naturally across the upper back area.
- Select a towel pattern that complements your brand identity, like tropical prints for vacation brands, geometric patterns for modern aesthetics, or solid colors for minimalist luxury appeal.
- Pick a swimwear style that aligns with your target audience, such as bikini bottoms for fashion brands, athletic cuts for fitness products, or high-waisted styles for retro-inspired campaigns, and use the JSON prompt to guide the AI image creation.
