### What it does

- Generates a 3×2 grid AI image showcasing a travel product through six distinct lifestyle and detail panels.

### Tips

- Choose a specific product name like "Voyager Pro Suitcase" or "Urban Nomad Backpack" and match travel contexts to your target audience, such as airport terminals for business travelers or train stations for urban commuters.
- Define a cohesive color palette that reflects your brand identity, using 2-3 complementary colors like navy and copper for premium appeal or sage green and cream for eco-conscious positioning.
- Use the JSON prompt to guide the AI in creating a professional 3×2 grid layout, specifying realistic travel destinations like "cobblestone European street" or "modern Asian airport lounge" to bring each panel to life.
