### What it does

- Generates a street photography AI image with a subject in statement wardrobe positioned on a busy urban street.
- Applies golden hour lighting with shallow depth of field to create a Vogue-style editorial portrait.
- Produces ultra-realistic details with sharp subject focus and soft blurred city background for cinematic atmosphere.
