### 2. Roast Me Reflection

**Use Case:** Use humor and directness to expose habits and generate practical next actions.

**Target Personas:** Individual Contributor, Professional, Student

**Tags:** `Self-Assessment`, `Self-Improvement`, `Reflection`

**Prompt:**

```
Roast me on [topic] with 4 to 5 sharp but fair points.
Then provide:
1) One-line summary of my current pattern,
2) One practical next step I can execute this week.
Tone: witty, direct, but useful.
```
