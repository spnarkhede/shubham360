### 3. Affirmation and Growth

**Use Case:** Combine confidence-building reflection with practical self-improvement actions.

**Target Personas:** Individual Contributor, Student, Professional

**Tags:** `Self-Improvement`, `Affirmation`, `Reflection`

**Prompt:**

```
Give me two sections:
1) Affirmation: 4 to 5 bold points highlighting my strengths and resilience,
2) Growth reflection: 4 to 5 honest points on where I should improve.

Then provide:
- One-line summary,
- One concrete action plan for the next 7 days.
Tone: supportive, direct, practical.
```
