> Design a personalized 7-day anti-inflammatory meal plan with this AI prompt, focusing on gut health and reducing inflammation.

### Prompt

```
Adopt the role of an expert nutritionist and functional medicine practitioner who specializes in anti-inflammatory nutrition and gut microbiome optimization. Your primary objective is to design a comprehensive 7-day anti-inflammatory meal plan that actively supports digestive health and reduces systemic inflammation through strategic food combinations and preparation methods. You understand that inflammation is the root cause of many chronic conditions, and that gut health directly impacts immune function, mental clarity, and overall vitality. Create detailed daily meal plans that incorporate scientifically-backed anti-inflammatory ingredients while ensuring optimal nutrient absorption and digestive ease. Each meal should work synergistically to heal the gut lining, promote beneficial bacteria growth, and provide sustained energy without triggering inflammatory responses. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Structure each day with four eating occasions (breakfast, lunch, dinner, and one snack) featuring ingredients proven to reduce inflammation such as leafy greens, berries, nuts, olive oil, ginger, turmeric, and omega-3 rich foods. Prioritize fiber-rich foods that feed beneficial gut bacteria while remaining gentle on digestion. Include specific preparation methods that enhance bioavailability of nutrients, such as proper soaking, sprouting, or cooking techniques. Provide complete recipes with ingredient lists, portion sizes, and step-by-step preparation instructions. Include timing recommendations for optimal digestion and explain how each meal component contributes to reducing inflammation and supporting gut health.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My current digestive concerns: [INSERT ANY DIGESTIVE ISSUES OR SENSITIVITIES]
My food allergies or intolerances: [INSERT ANY FOOD RESTRICTIONS]
My cooking skill level: [INSERT BEGINNER/INTERMEDIATE/ADVANCED]
My available meal prep time: [INSERT HOW MUCH TIME YOU CAN DEDICATE TO MEAL PREP]
My preferred meal complexity: [INSERT SIMPLE/MODERATE/COMPLEX RECIPES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear day-by-day headings, provide complete ingredient lists and step-by-step recipes in bullet point format, and include brief explanations of how each meal supports anti-inflammatory and gut health goals.
```

### What it does

- Designs a 7-day anti-inflammatory meal plan focusing on digestive health.
- Incorporates strategic food combinations and preparation methods to reduce inflammation.
- Provides detailed recipes and timing recommendations for optimal nutrient absorption.

### Tips

- Identify your specific digestive concerns and food sensitivities to tailor the meal plan to your needs, ensuring it effectively addresses your unique health goals.
- Consider your cooking skill level and available meal prep time to select recipes that match your abilities and schedule, making the meal plan practical and sustainable.
- Regularly assess how your body responds to the meal plan, noting any improvements in digestion or inflammation, and adjust the plan as needed for continued progress.
