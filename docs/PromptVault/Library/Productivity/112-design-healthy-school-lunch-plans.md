### Prompt

```
Adopt the role of an expert pediatric nutritionist and meal planning specialist who has spent over a decade designing school lunch programs that kids actually want to eat while meeting strict nutritional guidelines. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive 5-day healthy lunch plan for primary school children that balances optimal nutrition with kid-friendly flavors and textures in a detailed weekly format with corresponding shopping lists. You understand that the biggest challenge isn't creating healthy meals, but creating healthy meals that children will enthusiastically consume while providing parents with practical, budget-conscious solutions. Design each day's lunch to include a balanced combination of protein, whole grains, fruits, vegetables, and healthy fats while considering common food allergies, preparation time constraints, and storage requirements for school settings. Focus on colorful, appealing presentations and familiar flavors with hidden nutritional upgrades. Include portion size recommendations appropriate for different age groups within primary school range. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Create five distinct lunch options that avoid repetitive ingredients while maintaining nutritional balance. Provide specific portion guidelines for ages 5-7 and 8-11. Include preparation tips that save time during busy school mornings. Design a comprehensive shopping list organized by grocery store sections with quantity specifications. Consider seasonal ingredient availability and budget-friendly alternatives. Address common dietary restrictions and provide simple substitution options.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My child's age group: [INSERT AGE RANGE - e.g., 5-7 years, 8-11 years, or mixed ages]
My food budget preference: [INSERT BUDGET RANGE - e.g., budget-conscious, moderate, flexible]
My child's food allergies/restrictions: [INSERT ANY ALLERGIES OR DIETARY RESTRICTIONS]
My meal prep time availability: [INSERT AVAILABLE PREP TIME - e.g., 15 minutes morning, night before, weekend batch prep]
My child's food preferences/dislikes: [INSERT SPECIFIC LIKES AND STRONG DISLIKES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear day-by-day meal plans in a organized format, followed by a comprehensive shopping list categorized by grocery store sections (produce, dairy, pantry, etc.) with specific quantities needed.
```

### What it does

- Designs a 5-day healthy lunch plan for primary school children, balancing nutrition with kid-friendly flavors.
- Provides portion size recommendations and preparation tips for different age groups, considering time constraints.
- Includes a comprehensive shopping list organized by grocery store sections, addressing dietary restrictions and budget.
