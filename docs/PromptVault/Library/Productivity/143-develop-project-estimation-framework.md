### What it does

- Provides a detailed framework for estimating task duration and effort in project management.
- Breaks down the estimation process into clear, actionable steps tailored to a specific industry.

### Tips

- Utilize historical data from past projects within the specified industry to establish a baseline for task durations and efforts, enhancing the accuracy of your estimates.
- Implement a dynamic estimation tool like Monte Carlo simulations to account for variability and uncertainty in task durations, ensuring more resilient planning.
- Regularly update your estimation techniques and tools based on the latest industry trends and technological advancements to maintain relevance and accuracy in your project estimations.
