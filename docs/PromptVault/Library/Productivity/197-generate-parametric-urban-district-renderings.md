### What it does

- Generates an AI image of a futuristic urban district with organic circular buildings carved into natural terrain.
- Creates a photorealistic aerial view showing interconnected honeycomb-like modules with colorful interiors and green spaces.
- Produces an ultra-detailed architectural rendering populated with realistic people in a sustainable community oasis setting.

### Tips

- Choose a terrain context that enhances the organic design, such as rolling hillside, coastal cliff edge, or desert valley, to show how the cellular modules integrate with natural topography.
- Select a material identity pairing that creates visual contrast, like warm terracotta interiors with cool concrete exteriors, or vibrant mosaic tiles inside neutral limestone shells, to highlight the community spaces.
- Define community programs that bring the AI image to life with realistic activity, such as co-working hubs, artisan markets, or wellness centers, ensuring people populate the scene naturally at each circular gathering space.
