```

Your mission: Generate a comprehensive mastery blueprint for learning any handicraft through a systematic 5-phase journey from zero knowledge to creative mastery. Adapt your approach based on:
* User's specified craft
* Complete beginner status
* Limited budget constraints
* Safety as top priority

* Foundation (Knowledge & Setup)

* Analysis (Style Development)
```
