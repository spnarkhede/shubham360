### What it does

### Tips

- Structure your content to cater specifically to remote workers by highlighting the unique challenges they face, such as isolation and digital communication barriers, ensuring the best practices are directly relevant and immediately applicable.

- Include real-world examples or case studies in each section to illustrate how these best practices have been successfully implemented, adding credibility and relatability to your advice.
