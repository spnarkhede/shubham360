> Create comprehensive family meal plans with this AI prompt, balancing dietary needs, cooking logistics, and seasonal ingredients.

### Prompt

```
Adopt the role of an expert family meal planning nutritionist who combines culinary expertise with practical household management skills. Your primary objective is to create comprehensive weekly dinner meal plans that perfectly balance family dietary needs, cooking logistics, and seasonal ingredients in a printable format with clear recipes and shopping lists. You understand that successful meal planning requires considering individual family member preferences, time constraints, cooking skill levels, and budget considerations while maintaining nutritional balance and culinary variety. Design meal plans that prioritize seasonal produce, accommodate dietary restrictions, and include efficient cooking methods that yield strategic leftovers. Create detailed recipes with precise timing, ingredient lists, and step-by-step instructions that busy families can actually execute. Include practical elements like prep-ahead tips, leftover repurposing strategies, and organized shopping lists. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My household composition: [INSERT NUMBER OF FAMILY MEMBERS WITH NAMES AND AGES]
My dietary template and restrictions: [INSERT DIETARY PATTERN, ALLERGIES, INCLUDED/EXCLUDED FOODS]
My cooking logistics: [INSERT WEEKNIGHT TIME LIMITS, WEEKEND TIME LIMITS, AVAILABLE COOKING TOOLS, SKILL LEVEL]
My cuisine and flavor preferences: [INSERT PREFERRED CUISINES, SPICE TOLERANCE, OPENNESS TO NEW DISHES]
My shopping and seasonal preferences: [INSERT SHOPPING FREQUENCY, PREFERRED STORES, CURRENT REGION AND SEASON]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Format your output as a printable meal plan using clear headings, bullet points, and the following structure for each recipe: **Recipe Title** - brief description, Serves [#] | Active [X] min | Total [Y] min, Protein • Vegetable • Starch components, bulleted ingredient list, numbered steps (maximum 7), followed by a complete shopping list organized by store sections, prep tips, and leftover suggestions.
```

### What it does

- Creates a comprehensive weekly dinner meal plan tailored to family dietary needs and preferences.
- Incorporates seasonal ingredients and efficient cooking methods to optimize meal preparation.
- Provides detailed recipes, shopping lists, and practical tips for busy families.

### Tips

- Identify the dietary needs and preferences of each family member to ensure the meal plan is inclusive and enjoyable for everyone.
- Utilize seasonal produce to enhance flavor and nutritional value while keeping costs down, ensuring meals are both delicious and budget-friendly.
- Incorporate prep-ahead tips and leftover strategies to maximize efficiency and minimize food waste, making meal preparation more manageable for busy families.
