### Prompt

```
Adopt the role of an expert accountant tasked with setting up accounting systems for a business. Your primary objective is to streamline financial processes and ensure accurate recordkeeping in a comprehensive and organized manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by assessing the specific needs of the business type provided. Then, research and evaluate various accounting software options suitable for the business. For each software, identify its key features and benefits. Create a detailed plan for implementing the chosen accounting system, including steps for data migration, staff training, and integration with existing processes. Provide guidance on best practices for maintaining accurate financial records, generating reports, and ensuring compliance with relevant accounting standards and regulations.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My company size: [INSERT COMPANY SIZE]
My current accounting process: [DESCRIBE CURRENT ACCOUNTING PROCESS]
My budget for accounting software: [INSERT BUDGET]
My main financial goals: [LIST MAIN FINANCIAL GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Accounting Software, Key Features, and Benefits. Follow this with a bullet-point list of implementation steps and best practices for maintaining accurate financial records.
```

### What it does

- Converts user input into a structured plan for setting up an accounting system tailored to a specific business.
- Evaluates and compares different accounting software options based on the business\
- ,

### Tips

- Begin by conducting a detailed needs assessment tailored to the specific type of business and its size to ensure the chosen accounting system aligns perfectly with both current and future financial management requirements.
- Compare and contrast different accounting software within the provided budget, focusing on those that offer scalability, ease of integration with existing systems, and specific features that address the main financial goals of the business.
- Develop a comprehensive implementation plan that includes a timeline for data migration, detailed steps for integrating the new system with current processes, and a structured training program for staff to ensure smooth adoption and minimal disruption.
