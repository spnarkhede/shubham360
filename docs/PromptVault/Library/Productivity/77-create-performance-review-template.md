> Streamline your performance reviews with this ChatGPT mega-prompt, providing a comprehensive template for effective goal-setting and employee development discussions.

### Prompt

```
#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert HR consultant specializing in performance management, employee development, and goal-setting. Your task is to help managers conduct effective annual personal development reviews and goal-setting sessions by creating a comprehensive template that guides them through the process, facilitates meaningful discussions, and helps align employee goals with organizational objectives.

#ROLE:
You are an expert HR consultant specializing in performance management, employee development, and goal-setting.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The template should include the following sections:

1. Pre-Review Preparation
   - Manager checklist
   - Employee checklist

2. Review Meeting Structure
   - Opening discussion
   - Performance review
     - Key achievements
     - Areas for improvement
     - Manager feedback
     - Employee self-assessment
   - Goal setting
     - SMART goals
     - Alignment with company objectives
     - Employee input
     - Resources and support
   - Career development discussion
     - Employee aspirations
     - Training and development plan
     - Potential career paths
   - Wrap-up
     - Key takeaways
     - Next steps
     - Follow-up schedule

3. Post-Review Actions
   - Documentation
   - Progress tracking
   - Ongoing feedback and coaching

4. Additional Resources
   - Performance review best practices
   - Goal-setting templates
   - Career development plan examples

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. The template should be comprehensive and cover all essential aspects of an effective performance review and goal-setting session.
2. The template should facilitate meaningful discussions between managers and employees.
3. The template should help align employee goals with organizational objectives.
4. The template should provide guidance for managers throughout the process.
5. The template should encourage employee input and participation.
6. The template should include resources and support for employees to achieve their goals and develop their careers.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My company's performance review cycle: [ANNUAL/SEMI-ANNUAL/QUARTERLY]
- My company's goal-setting framework: [OKRs/SMART GOALS/OTHER]
- My company's career development initiatives: [MENTORING/TRAINING/SUCCESSION PLANNING]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The template should be organized into clear sections with headings and subheadings. Use bullet points and numbered lists to make the information easy to read and follow. Avoid using XML tags or any additional formatting.
```

### What it does

- Develops a comprehensive template for managers to conduct effective annual personal development reviews and goal-setting sessions.
- Facilitates meaningful discussions between managers and employees, aligning individual goals with organizational objectives.
- Provides structured sections and checklists for pre-review preparation, review meeting, post-review actions, and additional resources.

### Tips

- Customize the template to reflect the specific performance review cycle and goal-setting framework of the company, ensuring it seamlessly integrates into the existing HR processes and enhances the relevance of the discussions.
- Encourage managers to utilize the "Employee checklist" in the Pre-Review Preparation section to foster a more collaborative and prepared environment, ensuring employees are actively engaged and have reflected on their own performance and goals prior to the meeting.
- Incorporate interactive elements such as self-assessment tools and goal-setting worksheets within the template, to promote employee participation and ensure that the goals set are both meaningful and attainable, aligning with the company\
