### Prompt

```
Develop a comprehensive AI ethics compliance checklist for advanced product teams, ensuring adherence to the highest ethical standards in AI system development. The checklist should systematically address key aspects of AI ethics,** including:** ## 1. **Data Privacy**: * Data collection and storage practices * User consent and data protection * Compliance with privacy regulations ## 2. **Bias and Fairness**: * Identifying and mitigating bias in AI models * Ensuring fairness in decision-making processes * Strategies for promoting diversity and inclusion ## 3. **Transparency and Explainability**: * Model interpretability techniques * Transparency in AI decision-making * Communicating AI outcomes to users ## 4. **Accountability and Governance**: * Establishing accountability frameworks * Governance structures for AI development * Addressing AI-related risks and liabilities ## 5. **User Rights and Autonomy**: * Respecting user autonomy and agency * Ensuring user control over AI-driven interactions * Protecting user rights in AI-mediated environments Include real-world examples and case studies to illustrate each point, providing actionable guidance for product teams. Ensure the checklist is structured for ease of use, with clear, concise language and a logical flow that facilitates thorough compliance with AI ethics standards.
```
