### Prompt

```

Ensure explanations are clear and concise, using relatable examples to demonstrate how AI can improve business operations and decision-making processes. Identify and explain the essential AI concepts that business leaders need to understand to make informed decisions about integrating AI into their organizations,** covering topics such as:** ## 1. Defining AI and its types ## 2. Understanding machine learning and deep learning ## 3. Recognizing AI applications in business ## 4. Addressing AI ethics and responsibility ## 5. Assessing AI readiness and implementation strategies Use accessible language and provide actionable insights that facilitate informed decision-making about AI adoption.
