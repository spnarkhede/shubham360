> Design a comprehensive introductory AI course for business professionals with little to no prior knowledge of AI

### Prompt

```
Design a comprehensive introductory AI course for business professionals with little to no prior knowledge of AI. The course should cover foundational AI concepts, practical business applications, and real-world case studies. Ensure the content is engaging, accessible, and provides a medium level of detail.** The course outline should include the following components:** ## 1.** Foundational AI concepts:** * Definition and history of AI * Types of AI (narrow, general, and superintelligence) * Key AI technologies (machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing) ## 2.** AI relevance to business:** * Current AI trends and future outlook * Business applications of AI (customer service, marketing, operations) * AI-driven business models and innovations ## 3.** Leveraging AI in business:** * Identifying AI opportunities and challenges * Building an AI strategy and roadmap * Implementing AI solutions and measuring success ## 4.** Practical applications and case studies:** * Real-world examples of AI in business (success stories and lessons learned) * Hands-on exercises and group discussions ## 5.** Key learning objectives:** * Understand the basics of AI and its relevance to business * Identify opportunities for AI adoption in their organization * Develop a basic AI strategy and roadmap ## 6.** Suggested syllabus:** * Course duration and format (online, in-person, or hybrid) * Module breakdown and topics * Recommended readings and resources ## 7.** Engagement and assessment strategies:** * Interactive lectures and discussions * Group projects and presentations * Quizzes and assignments Structure the course to provide a solid foundation in AI for business professionals, with a focus on practical applications and real-world examples.
```

### What it does

- Guides you through designing and training an AI model end-to-end.
- Covers data collection, feature engineering, model selection, and validation.
- Includes best practices for deployment, monitoring, and iterative improvement.

### Tips

- Provide context about your dataset size and quality upfront for tailored advice.
- Ask follow-up questions to drill into specific model selection or evaluation steps.
- Request code snippets or pseudocode when you need implementation-level detail.

### How to use

1. Paste the full prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, or your preferred AI model.
2. Describe your specific use case, dataset characteristics, and target outcome.
3. Follow the structured guidance step by step, iterating on each phase.
4. Ask follow-up questions to go deeper on model selection, training, or deployment.
