### 1. Deal and Discount Checker

**Use Case:** Find valid flight promo codes and remove fake or expired offers.

**Target Personas:** Budget Traveller, Frequent Traveller, Deal Hunter

**Tags:** `Travel`, `Research`, `Cost Optimization`

**Prompt:**

```
Search for active promo codes, flash offers, and airline discounts for [airline names].
Verify expiration dates and legitimacy, and remove offers that are outdated or unconfirmed.
```
