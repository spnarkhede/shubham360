### 4. Route Optimizer with Smart Connections

**Use Case:** Find affordable connecting routes while controlling layover and transit risk.

**Target Personas:** Budget Traveller, Business Traveller, Frequent Traveller

**Tags:** `Travel`, `Planning`, `Route Optimization`

**Prompt:**

```
Design alternative routes with 1 to 3 connections from [origin] to [destination] for less than [budget].
Prioritize layovers under [X hours] and avoid airports with high transit fees when possible.
```
