### 5. Fee Breakdown and Reduction Plan

**Use Case:** Understand and reduce ancillary airline charges before booking.

**Target Personas:** Budget Traveller, Frequent Traveller, Business Traveller

**Tags:** `Travel`, `Cost Optimization`, `Planning`

**Prompt:**

```
Detail all additional fees for this flight: baggage, seats, priority boarding, and payment charges.
Suggest legal ways to reduce each fee based on current fare rules.
```
