### 1. Funder Research Prompt

**Use Case**: When you need to deeply research a funder before writing a single word of your proposal

**Target Personas**: Grant writers, nonprofit managers, research directors, fundraising teams

**Tags**: `funder-research`, `RFP-analysis`, `strategy-foundation`, `Claude`

**Prompt**:

```
Research [funder name] to inform proposal development.

Provide:
1. Funding priorities and focus areas
2. Recent grants awarded (if available)
3. Preferred approaches and methodologies
4. Evaluation criteria and weighting
5. Reporting requirements
6. Known biases or preferences

Also identify:
- Language and terminology the funder uses
- What they consider innovative vs. standard
- Common reasons for rejection (if known)
- What makes their funded projects successful

This research shapes proposal strategy and language.
```
