### 1. Client Context Brief Prompt

**Use Case**: When you need to build a complete client brief before writing a single word of the proposal

**Target Personas**: Sales reps, account executives, business development, proposal managers

**Tags**: `client-brief`, `deal-context`, `proposal-foundation`, `ChatGPT`

**Prompt**:

```
Prepare proposal brief for [client name].

Client information:
- Company: [name, industry, size]
- Decision makers: [names, roles]
- Current situation: [where they are now]

The opportunity:
- Problem they're solving: [the challenge]
- Why now: [urgency or timing]
- What they've tried: [previous approaches]

Our positioning:
- Our solution: [what we're proposing]
- Why we're uniquely qualified: [our differentiator]
- Relevant experience: [similar work, references]

Competitive context:
- Who else are they considering: [competitors]
- Our advantage over them: [differentiator]
- Risk of losing: [what makes us vulnerable]

Win theme:
[One sentence that captures why we should win]

Key messages:
1. [Message]: [why it resonates]
2. [Message]: [why it resonates]

Deal economics:
- Proposed price: [if discussed]
- Payment terms: [if discussed]
- Timeline expectations: [if discussed]

This brief anchors the entire proposal.
```

### 3. Executive Summary Prompt

**Target Personas**: Sales reps, proposal writers, account executives

**Tags**: `executive-summary`, `opening-hook`, `decision-maker-ready`, `ChatGPT`

**Prompt**:

```
Write executive summary for [client name] proposal.

Client: [company name]
Their challenge: [the problem]

Our solution: [what we propose]
Investment: [amount]

Executive summary structure:

Situation:
[2 sentences on where they are now]

Complication:
[2 sentences on why the problem matters now]

Resolution:
[3-4 sentences on what we propose and why it works]

Differentiated value:
[2 sentences on why us specifically]

Investment summary:
[1-2 sentences on scope and investment]

Call to action:
[What happens next]

Total: 250-300 words. Make executives want to read more.
```
