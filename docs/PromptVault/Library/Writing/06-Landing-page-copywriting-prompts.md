### 1. Basic Headline Prompt

**Use Case**: When you need multiple headline variations fast — benefit-focused, curiosity, question, command, and social proof types

**Target Personas**: Marketers, growth hackers, startup founders, conversion specialists

**Tags**: `headlines`, `variations`, `A/B-ready`, `ChatGPT`

**Prompt**:

```
Generate landing page headlines for:

Product: [what you're selling]
Target audience: [who will read this]
Primary benefit: [main value you provide]

Headline types to generate:
1. Benefit-focused: [leads with outcome]
2. Curiosity-gap: [creates intrigue]
3. Question: [addresses reader need]
4. Command: [directs action]
5. Social proof: [implies popularity]

Requirements:
- Under 10 words
- Specific not vague
- Clear benefit promise
- Matches your brand voice

Generate headlines that stop visitors.
```

### 2. Value-First Headline Prompt

**Use Case**: When you want headlines that lead with the outcome the visitor gets — not what your product does

**Target Personas**: Copywriters, CRO specialists, product marketers

**Tags**: `value-first`, `outcome-led`, `benefit-headline`, `ChatGPT`

**Prompt**:

```
Create headlines leading with value:

Product: [name and description]
Value proposition: [what makes you different]

Headline formula:
"[Specific outcome] without [common pain]" or
"[Specific outcome] in [timeframe]" or
"[Number] [specific benefits]"

Examples for inspiration:
- "Close deals 50% faster without the awkward pitch"
- "Launch in days, not months"
- "10x Your Email Response Rate"

Generate value-first headlines.
```

### 4. Value Proposition Prompt

**Use Case**: When you need a clear, formula-based value proposition that explains exactly who it's for and why it's different

**Target Personas**: Founders, product marketers, strategists, positioning experts

**Tags**: `value-proposition`, `positioning`, `formula-based`, `ChatGPT`

**Prompt**:

```
Write value proposition for:

Product: [what you offer]
target user: [who it's for]
Main benefit: [primary value]
Differentiator: [why you're different]

Value proposition formula:
"For [target user] who [has problem], [your product] is [category] that [delivers benefit], unlike [competitor or alternative] because [your differentiator]."

Example: "For sales teams who need accurate forecasts, Forecastly is a CRM that predicts deal outcomes using AI, unlike Salesforce because our AI actually learns from your closed deals."

Generate using this formula.
```
