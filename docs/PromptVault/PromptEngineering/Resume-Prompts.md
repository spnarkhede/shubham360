# Resume Prompts

A curated collection of AI prompts for optimizing, auditing, and tailoring your resume — from quick fixes to full-scale ATS transformation.

---

### 1. Professional Summary Rewrite

**Use Case:** Rewriting a professional summary to be concise, engaging, and aligned with a target job title and industry

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Resume Writing`, `Summary Rewrite`

**Prompt:**

```
I'm applying for a [Job Title] position in [Industry], and I want my resume summary to stand out. Please rewrite this professional summary to make it more concise, engaging, and aligned with the expectations for this role. It should reflect my strengths, relevant skills, and years of experience in a compelling way.
```

---

### 2. Accomplishment-Focused Bullet Points

**Use Case:** Reworking experience bullets to be results-oriented with measurable metrics instead of listing generic responsibilities

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Resume Writing`, `Impact Statements`

**Prompt:**

```
Here are the bullet points for one of my past roles. Please rework them to focus on measurable accomplishments rather than generic responsibilities. Make them results-oriented, use strong action verbs, and include numbers or metrics wherever possible.
```

---

### 3. ATS Keyword Optimization

**Use Case:** Tailoring resume content to include relevant keywords from a specific job posting to pass ATS filters while remaining readable

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Resume Writing`, `ATS Optimization`

**Prompt:**

```
I want my resume to pass ATS filters and still read well to human recruiters. Based on this job description [paste job description], can you help me optimize my resume content to include relevant keywords and phrases from the posting in a natural way?
```

---

### 4. Career Transition Resume Rewrite

**Use Case:** Reframing existing experience to highlight transferable skills when switching from one field to another without direct experience in the target role

**Target Personas:** Career Changer, Job Seeker

**Tags:** `Career Changer`, `Career Development`, `Transferable Skills`

**Prompt:**

```
I'm transitioning into a new career from [Previous Field] to [New Field]. Please help me rewrite my resume summary and key achievements so they highlight transferable skills and make a strong case for why I'm a great fit, even without direct experience.
```

---

### 5. Full Resume Audit

**Use Case:** Getting comprehensive feedback on tone, structure, vagueness, wordiness, and missed opportunities to show impact across an entire resume

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Analysis`, `Resume Audit`

**Prompt:**

```
Can you audit this entire resume and point out areas where I'm being too vague, too wordy, or not showing enough impact? I want your feedback on tone, structure, and how I can better emphasize leadership, results, or innovation.
```

---

### 6. Resume Format & Layout Optimization

**Use Case:** Restructuring a resume layout to be modern, scannable in under 10 seconds, with the most relevant experience prominently featured

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Resume Writing`, `Layout Design`

**Prompt:**

```
Please suggest a better format or layout for my resume that emphasizes my most recent experience and key skills while de-emphasizing older, less relevant roles. I want it to look modern and be easy to scan in under 10 seconds.
```

### 7. Work History Realignment

**Use Case:** Rewriting or restructuring work history bullets to match the language and core requirements of a specific target job description

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Individual Contributor

### 11. Resume Alignment Strategist

**Use Case:** Aligning a resume with a specific job description truthfully and defensibly, using a structured keyword match table and clarifying questions before suggesting changes

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Resume Writing`, `Keyword Alignment`

**Prompt:**

```
You are a Resume Alignment Strategist.

Your job is to align my resume with a job description — clearly, truthfully, and defensibly.

I will give you:

My resume (pasted below)

A job description (pasted below)

Using those two inputs, do the following:

Step 1: Extract Keywords Identify a concise list of high-priority hard and soft skills, tools, technologies, and role-specific terms from the job description. These should be specific phrases or expectations the job actually requires. Group them into:

Soft Skills

Hard Skills & Tools

Step 2: Match & Categorize For each keyword, organize the output in a table with these columns:

✅ / ⚠️ / ❌ (match status)

Keyword / Requirement

Where It Appears in My Resume — or if not, what action to take (ask clarifying question, add a new bullet, modify a bullet point, or do nothing)

Ask YES/NO clarifying questions where needed (e.g., if something is implied but not proven). If a keyword is only in my skills section, ask me for relevant context before suggesting how to work it into an experience bullet.

Be specific, structured, and easy to read. Do not invent or fluff anything — only suggest interview-defensible updates. Use clear and ATS-friendly bullet points in XYZ format where you suggest changes.

Your job is not to "enhance" — it's to truthfully align.

→ Once I answer the clarifying questions, show what resume changes to make by listing:

The original bullet (if any)

The updated bullet

Whether it's a new addition or a modification —------------------------------------—------------------------------------—----------------------------------------------
```

### 12. Senior Hiring Manager Evaluator

**Use Case:** Evaluating a resume through the lens of a senior hiring manager to understand what a top candidate looks like and where the current resume falls short

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Executive, Individual Contributor

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Analysis`, `Resume Audit`

**Prompt:**

```
Act as a senior hiring manager with over 20 years of experience in the [PREFERRED INDUSTRY]. You have firsthand expertise in the [DESIRED ROLE] and a deep understanding of what it takes to succeed in this position. Your task is to identify the ideal candidate based solely on their resume, ensuring they meet and exceed expectations for [JOB DESCRIPTION].

Break down the key qualifications, technical and soft skills, relevant experience, and project work that would make a candidate stand out. Highlight essential industry certifications, domain expertise, and the impact of past roles in shaping their suitability.

Additionally, evaluate leadership qualities, problem-solving abilities, and adaptability to evolving industry trends. If applicable, consider cultural fit, teamwork, and communication skills required for success in the organization.

Finally, provide a structured assessment framework what an exceptional resume should look like, red flags to avoid, and how to differentiate between a good candidate and a perfect hire. Ensure your response is comprehensive, strategic, and aligned with real-world hiring best practices.
```

### 14. ATS Friendly Resume Creator

**Use Case:** Running a deep, multi-step ATS keyword extraction, prioritization, integration strategy, resume audit, and final transformation into a fully optimized resume and cover letter

**Target Personas:** Job Seeker, Career Changer, Executive

**Tags:** `Job Seeker`, `Resume Writing`, `ATS Optimization`

**Prompt:**

```
Act as the most advanced, AI-powered Applicant Tracking System (ATS) combined with an elite executive-level resume strategist. Your mission is to conduct an exhaustive, hyper-detailed, line-by-line analysis of the provided [job description] leaving absolutely no detail, nuance, or implied requirement overlooked.

Step 1: Comprehensive Keyword & Skills Extraction

Meticulously extract and compile an all-encompassing list of:

• All explicit keywords and phrases stated directly in the job description

• All implicit keywords and behavioral competencies strongly implied but not directly stated

• Required and preferred technical skills, soft skills, leadership traits, and cultural fit qualities emphasized

• Certifications, software, tools, methodologies, frameworks, and industry-specific terminology

Step 2: Priority & Ranking

• Assign a priority ranking to each keyword and skill based on ATS algorithm scoring and human recruiter preferences

• Clearly highlight which keywords and competencies are absolute must-haves to pass automated ATS scans and deeply resonate with human hiring managers

Step 3: Integration Strategy & Phrasing

Provide precise, actionable strategies to naturally and effectively integrate these keywords into my resume and cover letter, including:

• Exact phrasing suggestions and variations to maintain authenticity

• Advanced measurable, results-driven impact statements showcasing each skill and responsibility

• Contextual storytelling approaches that weave keywords seamlessly and credibly

• Techniques to emphasize leadership, initiative, problem-solving, and cultural fit

• Methods to illustrate accomplishments using quantified results, metrics, and tangible outcomes

Step 4: Resume Audit & Optimization Preparation

Once this comprehensive keyword and integration analysis is complete, prompt me to upload my resume. Then:

• Conduct an intensive, line-by-line audit of my resume

• Identify every single gap, missing keyword, or under-optimized phrase

• Suggest targeted, ATS-optimized, and recruiter-appealing improvements for each section and bullet point

Ensure:

• No bullet point is left generic or vague

• All responsibilities and achievements are contextualized with measurable results and specific outcomes

• Every keyword from the job description is incorporated authentically and strategically

• Skills are grouped and formatted to maximize ATS parsing accuracy

• Soft skills and cultural fit signals are seamlessly integrated throughout

Step 5: Final Masterpiece

Transform my resume and cover letter into a flawlessly optimized, precision-engineered set of application materials that:

• Achieve a 100% ATS score

• Position me unmistakably as the top candidate in the eyes of any hiring manager

• Dramatically increase my chances of securing an interview

• Leave no doubt that I am the ideal candidate they have been searching for

________________________________________

Final Objective: Deliver a resume and cover letter so meticulously crafted that they become undeniable proof of my fit and potential — guaranteeing that I stand out in any competitive applicant pool.

________________________________________

Additionally, format my resume as follows:

Name and contact details at the top.

'PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY' section on its own line with a full paragraph summary.

'PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE' on its own line, followed by each job title on a separate line.

Company name on its own line.

Dates on their own line.

Each bullet point starts with a bullet symbol on a separate line from the text.

Include separate sections for 'PROJECTS', 'SKILLS', and 'EDUCATION', each on their own lines.

Maintain clear line breaks and consistent spacing exactly like the final formatted document I've provided.
```
